Analyst Business Intelligence
Luxembourg
Be the First to Apply
Job Description
Task and responsibilities
Develop and maintain recurring and ad hoc reports for internal and external stakeholders, including Sales reporting, Customer & Emission reporting as well as Operational Performance analysis.
Design and implement analytical models to support data-driven decision-making and provide actionable business insights.
Manage and maintain data flows across internal systems (e.g., SAP BW, SAP SAC), ensuring efficient and reliable data processing.
Collaborate with the IT department to define, develop, and enhance databases
Support the Business Intelligence and Controlling teams in budgeting and forecasting activities, contributing analytical input and data validation.
Ensure data integrity and accuracy across all reporting layers, including master data management, and validate newly developed datasets and reporting structures.
 
Requirements
University degree in a relevant field such as Economics, Finance, Accounting, or a related discipline.
Strong IT skills, with advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel (e.g., complex formulas, data analysis).
Knowledge of data processing and programming languages (e.g., Python, SQL, JavaScript); experience with SAP is considered an advantage.
Solid understanding of financial modeling and analytical concepts.
Ability to work independently on daily tasks in an effective and structured manner.
Strong communication skills, with the ability to interact with internal and external stakeholders.
Team-oriented mindset with a collaborative and proactive approach.
Fluency in written and spoken English is mandatory; additional languages such as German or French are considered an advantage.
 
Working Conditions
Full-time, permanent position 
Based in our HQ in Sandweiler, Luxembourg
