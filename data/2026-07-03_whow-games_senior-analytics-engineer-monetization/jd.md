Senior Analytics Engineer - Monetization (m/w/d)

Whow Games seeks an analytics professional to manage monetization data infrastructure. The role bridges data teams and monetization stakeholders, transforming player and revenue insights into strategic decisions for their social games portfolio.

Key Responsibilities:
The position involves owning the monetization measurement stack, building scalable dbt models and Tableau dashboards, and developing predictive models for player lifetime value and conversion rates. The engineer will partner with game balancing teams to evaluate in-game economies and communicate findings to senior leadership. Additional duties include designing A/B tests for pricing and offers, managing data collection specifications, and conducting payer segmentation analysis.

Required Qualifications:
Candidates need 4+ years in monetization analytics or gaming analytics roles. Technical skills include proficiency in SQL, Python, dbt, and Tableau. Deep understanding of free-to-play monetization mechanics: IAP funnels, offer design, pricing, and live ops is essential. A/B testing experience, stakeholder communication abilities, and fluent English are required.

Preferred Qualifications:
Ideal candidates bring gaming industry backgrounds, game economy modeling expertise, and familiarity with payment processors like Xsolla or Stripe. Experience with hybrid monetization models and AI tools strengthens applications. German fluency is advantageous.

Benefits:
The company offers professional development, flexible schedules with remote options (two office days weekly), company events, meal allowances, transit subsidies, and wellness memberships.
