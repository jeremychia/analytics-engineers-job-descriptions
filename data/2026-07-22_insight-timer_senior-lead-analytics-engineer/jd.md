Senior / Lead Analytics Engineer - Insight Timer
Location: Melbourne, Victoria, Australia

Company Description:
Insight Timer describes itself as "the world's largest meditation and wellness community, with over 30 million users." The company's mission centers on building a platform that promotes health and wellness rather than exploiting user engagement like social media platforms.

This is described as a "hands-on role with a lot of ownership" where the successful candidate will "measure and interpret the experiments that decide what we ship" and "build the data models and metrics the whole company relies on."

Key Responsibilities:

- "Analytics delivery. Turn questions from the product team and company leaders into clear, useful answers"
- "Data ingestion, modelling and metrics. Own how data comes in, how it's modelled, and the metrics people trust"

- "Mentoring and leadership" including "directly mentoring and developing other analytics engineers"

- "Excellent communication and interpersonal skills"
- "Solid experience as an analytics engineer and owning work end to end"
- "Strong SQL on a modern cloud warehouse. We use BigQuery, Looker and dbt"
- "Experience with large, event-based datasets with varied schemas"
- "A track record of analysing and interpreting A/B tests"

Benefits:
- "Competitive salaries and options"
- "Fully remote" with Australia-based requirement
- "Extra leave days" and "generous L&D budget"
