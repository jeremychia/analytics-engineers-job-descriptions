Analytics Engineer - London at Fresha

Location: London (4 days onsite required)

Salary: £58-80k per annum (based on experience)

Department: Analytics

Role Overview

Fresha seeks an Analytics Engineer to join its global team, reporting to the VP Analytics. The role involves collaborating with Product Managers, Engineers, Analysts, and Commercial Leadership to model data, ensuring clean and accurate information for both decision-making and product functionality.

Key Responsibilities

- Develop, test, and maintain dimensional data models from diverse sources including CDC data, APIs, and SFTPs
- Enhance data model efficiency and performance while improving accessibility
- Build and maintain high-quality internal and external data products
- Advise product teams on analytical requirements for new features
- Analyze feature performance and support product operations through queries and dashboards
- Provide commercial and finance teams with accurate, accessible business data
- Create comprehensive data documentation for team consistency and AI tool effectiveness

Required Qualifications

- Expert SQL proficiency
- Experience building and testing data modeling pipelines
- Understanding of data platform and warehousing concepts
- Proficiency with modern data stack tools (dbt mandatory, cloud warehouse required)
- Experience using AI development tools in workflows
- Interest in optimizing data model performance
- Proactive, autonomous problem-solving approach

Bonus Qualifications

- Product decision-driving experience
- Familiarity with Fivetran, Airflow, Snowflake, Plotly, Starrocks, or Metabase
- ELT pipeline experience with SQL and Python
- Claude Code and MCP experience
- Fast-paced startup environment background
