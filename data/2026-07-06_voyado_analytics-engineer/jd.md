Company: Voyado
Job Title: Analytics Engineer
Department: Product Development - Engage
Location: Stockholm
Office Policy: Office First

Key Details

Reporting to: Robin Bodin, Engineering Manager

Role Description
The position focuses on building data foundations for analytics and AI capabilities. The posting states: "This is not a traditional BI or dashboarding role."

Primary responsibilities include designing governed data products, writing SQL for transformation and validation, building semantic definitions and certified metrics, collaborating with domain teams, and supporting embedded BI and future AI analytics.

Required Qualifications
- Strong SQL skills for data exploration, transformation, and validation
- Experience with dbt or similar tooling
- Background building data products, semantic layers, or certified metrics
- Understanding of lineage, data contracts, canonical entities, and quality testing
- Familiarity with modern platforms (Databricks, Snowflake, BigQuery)

Preferred: Retail tech, e-commerce, Databricks, or lakehouse platform experience

Benefits
30 days vacation, bike lease, Friday breakfast, in-house gym, gadget package, 5000 SEK annual wellness allowance, parental leave support, and weekly wellness hour.
