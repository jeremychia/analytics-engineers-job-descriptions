Business Intelligence & Data Analytics

Network For Electronic Transfers (S) (NETS)
Location: Braddell, Singapore

The role focuses on creating and maintaining departmental dashboards while consolidating data from multiple sources. The analyst will translate business requirements into clear KPIs and visualisations and perform trend analysis to support management decisions. Additional duties include monitoring performance metrics, ensuring data quality across reports, and engaging with stakeholders to clarify reporting needs.

Requirements

Candidates should hold a Bachelor's degree in business, Analytics, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field. The posting specifies 5-8 years of overall experience with at least 3-5 years dedicated to data analysis or business intelligence roles.

Technical competencies include hands-on expertise with BI platforms like Power BI or Tableau, plus SQL proficiency. Preferred qualifications mention data modeling and DAX experience. Soft skills emphasize analytical thinking, communication abilities, and the capacity to translate complex data into clear insights for non-technical stakeholders.
