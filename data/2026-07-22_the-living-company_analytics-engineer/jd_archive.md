WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2013 as Scape Australia, leaders in student housing, we've since grown into a vertically integrated living platform known as The Living Company; managing $17B in assets across Student Living (Scape), Rent to Live – Affordable Housing (RTL Co.), and Retirement Living (Aveo); spanning site origination, investment, planning, design, development, construction, and operations.

The Living Company is reshaping the way Australians live by delivering sustainable, community-driven, and high-quality spaces across the country.

THE ROLE

Ready to build the data foundation that powers decisions for 10,000+ retirement living residents across Australia? As our new Analytics Engineer, you'll be at the centre of a greenfield Snowflake deployment, designing and building the pipelines, models and data products the whole business will run on.

This isn't a maintenance role. You'll shape how data flows from source systems through to Finance, Sales, Marketing and Operations reporting, working directly with stakeholders to understand what they need and translating that into clean, reliable, well-documented dbt models.

Day-to-day you will be (but not limited to):

Contributing to the Snowflake data platform deployment, building out infrastructure from the ground up
Migrating legacy data infrastructure into Snowflake across multiple business domains
Building and maintaining dbt models across bronze, silver, gold and platinum layers, writing clean, tested and well-documented SQL
Building and maintaining data ingestion into Snowflake via Fivetran and Snowpark-based pipelines
Turning raw source data into trusted, analytics-ready data products the business can rely on
Working directly with Finance, CRM and operational teams to gather and refine data requirements
Translating business problems and ambiguous requirements into clear, scalable data solutions
Monitoring and maintaining data pipeline health, troubleshooting issues and ensuring reliability
Contributing to emerging AI data initiatives as the platform matures
Staying close to evolving best practice in the modern data stack (Snowflake, dbt, Fivetran) and bringing new ideas back to the team
