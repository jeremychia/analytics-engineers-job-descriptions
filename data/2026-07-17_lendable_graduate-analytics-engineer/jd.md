About Lendable

Lendable is a fintech unicorn focused on helping people access credit and save money. The company has achieved significant milestones including being "one of the UK's newest unicorns with a team of just over 700 people" and is "profitable since 2017." They serve the UK and US markets across loans, credit cards, and car finance products.

Role Overview

This junior position supports Lendable's US Cards team, focusing on building the analytical foundation. You'll collaborate with analysts, product teams, backend engineers, and stakeholders to improve data structuring, transformation, and consumption.

Key Responsibilities

- Contribute to data models supporting credit decisions, origination, portfolio analysis, and investor reporting
- Build and improve dbt models and transformations with senior engineer guidance
- Bridge communication between analysts, engineers, product teams, and data platform team
- Identify opportunities to improve transformation pipeline efficiency and reliability
- Support data infrastructure scaling

Required Qualifications

- Solid SQL skills with commitment to continuous improvement
- Hands-on dbt or ELT pipeline experience
- Collaborative communication style across technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Growing understanding of data modeling principles
- Comfort using AI tools

Desirable Qualifications

- Snowflake or cloud data warehouse experience
- Interest in supporting analysts through shared patterns
- Fintech or scale-up background

Tech Stack
