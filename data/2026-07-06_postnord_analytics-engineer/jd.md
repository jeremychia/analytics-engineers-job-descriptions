# Analytics Engineer at PostNord, Stockholm

Company: PostNord Sverige
Location: Solna, Stockholm (Terminalvägen 24, Solna)
Job Title: Analytics Engineer
Employment Type: Individual Pay
Application Deadline: July 12, 2026
Published: June 12, 2026

Job Description
PostNord seeks an Analytics Engineer to join their newly established Data & Analytics team. The role focuses on converting daily data from millions of parcels and business decisions into actionable insights and practical AI applications.

Key Responsibilities
- Develop data products, analytics solutions, and AI-driven capabilities with business stakeholders
- Enable analysts and business users to work independently with data, reporting, and analytical tools
- Improve data flows and define requirements for integrations from new data sources
- Drive initiatives strengthening PostNord's data-driven capabilities

Required Qualifications
- Several years of experience as an Analytics Engineer or similar position
- Strong stakeholder collaboration skills and ability to translate requirements into data solutions
- Knowledge of data modeling methodologies: "Kimball, Data Vault and Medallion architectures"
- Proficiency in SQL and Python
- Experience with Power BI, including DAX, Power Query, and semantic models
- Hands-on cloud platform experience: Azure, Microsoft Fabric, or Databricks
- Familiarity with modern development practices: Git, CI/CD pipelines, database security
- Relevant degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Intelligence, or related field
- Professional English proficiency; Swedish considered advantageous

Benefits
- Flexible workplace
- Union contract
- Professional development
- Access to new technologies
