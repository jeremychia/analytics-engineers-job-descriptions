# Analytics Engineer Position at Bravida

Company: Bravida
Job Title: Analytics Engineer
Location: Stockholm
Salary: Not specified in posting

Key Responsibilities
The role centers on data modeling using dbt and Snowflake. The engineer will "design and own the semantic layer that makes Bravida's operational and financial data trustworthy, consistent, and accessible." Additional duties include managing Dagster pipelines, establishing modeling standards, leveraging AI tools, and advancing generative analytics capabilities.

Required Qualifications
Candidates need 3+ years in analytics or data engineering roles with proven dbt expertise in production environments. Snowflake proficiency, dimensional modeling knowledge, and familiarity with data contracts are essential. The posting emphasizes "Active user of AI tools in your workflow" and interest in generative BI applications.

Tools & Technology Stack
- dbt (primary deliverable)
- Snowflake
- Dagster (pipeline management)
- AI tooling integration

Team & Culture
The Data & Analytics team operates within Operational Excellence, described as "fast-moving" with direct stakeholder engagement across operations, procurement, and finance across Nordic regions.

Hiring Process
The company conducts an introductory call, practical technical case study, team meeting, and stakeholder discussions.

Note: Candidates must be eligible to work in Sweden and the EU.
