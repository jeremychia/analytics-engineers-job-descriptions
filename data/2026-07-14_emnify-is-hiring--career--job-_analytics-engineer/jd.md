Why join our
Engineering team?
Team & structure
Our engineering team is made up of engineers primarily based in our Berlin and Würzburg offices. The team is organised into cross-functional squads aligned to product domains, bringing together backend, frontend, and infrastructure expertise.
Business impact
Engineering builds and scales the platform that powers global IoT connectivity. Their work enables customers across industries—from aviation and robotics to smart cities and beyond—to connect, manage, and scale their devices reliably, while driving emnify’s growth and product innovation.
What you’ll work on
You’ll tackle complex technical challenges across distributed systems, connectivity, and APIs—building scalable solutions and shipping features that have real-world impact at global scale.
Why join our
Finance
team?
Team & structure
Our finance team is a small, lean group primarily based in Berlin, covering FP&A, accounting, and commercial finance. The team works closely with leadership and partners directly with departments across the business.
Business impact
Finance sits at the centre of decision-making—providing the insights that guide how we invest, grow, and scale. From supporting international expansion to shaping commercial strategy, their work has a direct impact on emnify’s trajectory.
What you’ll work on
You’ll work on forecasting, budgeting, and commercial analysis, partnering with teams across the business to understand performance, challenge assumptions, and help drive smarter decisions.
Why join our
Go-to-market
team?
Team & structure
Our go-to-market team is made up of people across four continents. Each major region—LATAM, the US, Europe, and the Philippines—has dedicated sales, customer success, and marketing specialists working closely together.
Business impact
The team drives new customer acquisition, builds brand awareness, and develops strong, long-term customer relationships. They play a key role in expanding our global presence and shaping how emnify is positioned in the market.
What you’ll work on
You’ll build relationships with both new and existing customers, develop a deep understanding of market needs, and help uncover opportunities that inform new product development. Your work will directly contribute to expanding our global footprint.
Why join our
Legal
team?
Team & structure
Legal is a small, highly collaborative team working across commercial, regulatory, and compliance areas. The team operates globally, partnering closely with teams across the business to support a wide range of initiatives.
Business impact
Legal enables the business to scale internationally while managing risk in a complex regulatory landscape. Their work supports customers across diverse industries—from smart cities and aviation to EV charging and beyond—ensuring we can operate confidently across markets.
What you’ll work on
You’ll work on a broad range of legal challenges, from commercial contracts to regulatory initiatives and strategic projects. It’s a fast-paced, agile environment where no two problems are the same, shaped by the diversity of our customers and the global nature of our business.
Why join our
People Ops
team?
Team & structure
People Ops partners with teams across the business, spanning hiring, development, and culture. Working across 15+ countries and four major hubs—São Paulo, Berlin, the United States, and Manila—the team brings together a wide range of working styles and cultural perspectives.
Business impact
The team builds the foundation for a high-performing, inclusive, and globally connected organisation. By bringing together diverse cultures and ways of working, they enable teams to collaborate effectively and perform at their best.
What you’ll work on
You’ll shape hiring strategies, employee experience, and development programs across a globally distributed workforce—helping to build a cohesive culture while supporting teams in different regions to thrive.
Why join our
Product management
team?
Team & structure
Our product managers are primarily based in Germany and across Europe, working closely with engineering and design in cross-functional teams aligned to key product domains.
Business impact
Product sits at the intersection of customer needs, technology, and business strategy. The team defines what we build and why—shaping the platform to support customers across industries like fleet management, EV charging, aviation, pet tracking, and more, while driving growth and differentiation for emnify.
What you’ll work on
You’ll lead product discovery, prioritisation, and delivery—working closely with customers and internal teams to identify opportunities, define solutions, and bring new features to market that solve real-world connectivity challenges at scale.
Your benefits
Work flexibility
We're a globally distributed team, making flexibility essential. Our roles are designed as hybrid, remote, or include relocation opportunities.
Time to recharge
Enjoy a generous time-off policy starting at 30 days (location-based) to recharge and focus on what matters most.
Learning support
Ongoing professional development opportunities through access to courses, certifications, or conferences.
Good vibes
Join regular company events and team outings to connect with colleagues, celebrate, and have fun. Oh, and did we mention our dog-friendly offices?
Our hiring process
With our skills-first assessment, we focus on what you can do and the strengths you bring to the table, giving you a fair chance to shine based on your abilities, not just your resume.
1.
Application Review
Our Talent Partners will review your application and skills for the current role and future opportunities.
2.
Talent Interview
A 45-minute chat with one of our Talent Partners to learn about your skills, motivation and achievements.
3.
Department Interview
A 1-hour discussion with your Hiring Manager, who will evaluate your skills and expertise. Let your skills shine and show us how you can contribute!
4.
Functional Assessment
Be ready to demonstrate your skills through tasks like case studies, presentations, panel discussions, or coding challenges (depending on the role).
5.
Final interview
Get to know your team and stakeholders. Discuss your fit and culture- add with us, and how we can grow together!
We believe in giving everyone a fair chance!
