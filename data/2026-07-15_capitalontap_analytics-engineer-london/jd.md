Analytics Engineer at Capital on Tap

Location: London (Hybrid: 1-2 days per week in office)

Salary: Not specified

Company Context

Capital on Tap is a fintech platform serving small businesses with credit cards, spend management tools, and financial products. The company operates at scale with over 1,000 employees and 200,000+ customers.

Role Overview

The Analytics Engineer will be part of the Data Platform team, responsible for building and maintaining the modern data infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

- Managing the global data stack (Fivetran, Snowflake, dbt, Looker)
- Designing and maintaining data marts using dbt models
- Creating custom data pipelines with Python and Azure
- Building complex datasets for business requirements
- Implementing process improvements via CI/CD and automation
- Collaborating with Executive, Risk, Commercial, and Engineering teams

Required Qualifications

- Expert SQL and data modeling knowledge (e.g., Kimball)
- Hands-on dbt and Git experience
- ELT tools (Fivetran) and cloud data warehouse (Snowflake) expertise
- BI tool familiarity (Looker, Omni, Tableau, or PowerBI)
- Strong analytical and interpersonal skills

Interview Process

Three stages—30-minute values call, 45-minute CV review, and technical assessment with team leads (60 minutes each)

Benefits

- Private healthcare
- Travel insurance
- 28 days holiday
- Pension matching (7%)
- Learning budget
- Enhanced parental leave
- Therapy sessions
- Dog-friendly offices
