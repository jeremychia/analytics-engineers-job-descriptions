Senior Analytics Engineer - Rebtel Job Posting

Position: Senior Analytics Engineer
Department: Data
Location: Stockholm

Role Overview

"As a Senior Analytics Engineer at Rebtel, you are the person who makes data trustworthy and usable at scale." The role involves designing and maintaining data transformations, owning the semantic layer, and ensuring data quality across the organization.

Key Responsibilities

- Design and maintain dbt transformations powering self-service analytics
- Own Rebtel's semantic layer with LookML and Looker platform administration
- Enforce data quality through testing, validation, and monitoring
- Drive data documentation and literacy
- Optimize query performance on Snowflake
- Partner with Data Engineer on modeling standards
- Work with business stakeholders to create data products
- Stay current with analytics engineering best practices

Requirements

- 5+ years in Analytics/Data Engineering
- Strong SQL and hands-on dbt production experience
- Semantic layer ownership with LookML and Looker preferred
- Dimensional modeling knowledge
- Snowflake or similar cloud data warehouse familiarity
- Git and CI/CD workflow experience
- Excellent documentation and communication skills

Company Benefits

Rebtel offers pension, health insurance, dental coverage, wellness allowance (5,000 SEK), gym discounts, bonus program, parental pay, 30 days vacation, and relocation support.

About Rebtel: Founded in 2006, the 73-person company operates from Stockholm with a mission connecting diaspora communities globally through affordable international calling services.
