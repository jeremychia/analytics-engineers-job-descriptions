Analytics Engineer Position at Datashift NV

Company: Datashift NV
Job Title: Analytics Engineer
Location: Mechelen, Belgium
Salary: Not specified

Key Responsibilities
The role involves translating client requirements into sustainable data solutions. Specific duties include "designing data solutions, balancing cost and maintainability" and collaborating with data scientists and business consultants. Additional responsibilities encompass monitoring data quality, staying current with industry developments, and mentoring junior team members.

Required Qualifications
Candidates should have 4-6 years of experience with at least 2 years in cloud environments. Essential skills include:
- Advanced SQL proficiency
- Expertise with dbt for data transformation projects
- Familiarity with cloud data warehouses and data modeling approaches
- DevOps knowledge, including "CI/CD, IaC, and unit testing"
- Analytical, solution-oriented mindset with agile experience
- Fluency in Dutch or French plus English

Preferred Qualifications
Docker, PySpark, and Python dataframe libraries (Pandas, Polars) experience is advantageous.

Benefits
The company offers competitive employment terms including vehicle allowance, meal vouchers, bonuses, professional development opportunities, and growth potential within an "entrepreneurial team" focused on quality delivery.
