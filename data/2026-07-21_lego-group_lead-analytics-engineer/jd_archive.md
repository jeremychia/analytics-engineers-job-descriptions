# Lead Analytics Engineer

**Company:** LEGO Group
**Job Title:** Lead Analytics Engineer
**Locations:** Billund, Denmark; London, United Kingdom
**Management Level:** Senior Manager
**Job ID:** 0000035035
**Category:** Data & Analytics

## Core Responsibilities

- Lead a team of 5 Analytics Engineers & Associate Analytics Engineers in the Analytics Engineering Delivery sub-team
- Collaborate with Analytics Interface, Commercial Analytics, and business teams to convert requirements into AI-enabling data product backlog
- Partner with Analytics Innovation & Automation and Data Office Product teams on roadmap delivery for cutting-edge features
- Ensure data products follow CI/CD best practices, adhere to data quality frameworks with assertion checks, and apply performance optimization
- Drive Unity Catalog governance including schemas, access, and metadata tagging for improved data accessibility
- Coach team members on data understanding and champion data literacy through training and documentation
- Drive commercial data availability by collaborating with digital product teams to ensure quality data collection
- Champion best practices in data product development across the organization

## Requirements

**Essential:**
- Considerable Analytics Engineering, Data Solutions, Data Engineering and/or Data Specialist experience
- Proven technical team leadership experience
- Expert SQL and/or Python skills for manipulating large datasets
- CI/CD pipeline and Git experience with data transformation knowledge
- Ability to translate technical constraints to non-technical stakeholders
- Detail-oriented with strong adaptability
- Collaborative mindset with positive attitude
- Willingness to travel 10 days annually
- Fluent English (verbal and written)

**Nice to Have:**
- Cross-cultural global collaboration experience
- AGILE methodology familiarity
- Commercial compliance knowledge (Competition Law, GDPR, Confidentiality)

- Colleague discount from day one
