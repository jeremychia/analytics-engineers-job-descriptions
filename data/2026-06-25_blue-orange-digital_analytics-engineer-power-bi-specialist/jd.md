## Position Overview

Blue Orange Digital seeks a Senior Analytics Engineer specialising in Power BI to lead the analytics and visualisation components of a 26-week enterprise data warehouse implementation in commodities trading. The role runs weeks 6-22 full-time, focusing on designing Fabric Semantic Models and production dashboards for Trader P&L, Global Position, Risk Metrics (VaR), and Financial Consolidation reporting.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and construct Microsoft Fabric Semantic Models using Gold-layer data
- Create DAX measures, hierarchies, relationships, and semantic structures
- Develop operational dashboards for trading, risk, and finance teams
- Engage directly with stakeholders to translate requirements into technical designs
- Optimize DirectQuery and Import performance for financial datasets
- Establish naming conventions and governance standards
- Collaborate with Data Engineers on analytics-ready outputs
- Support user enablement and UAT validation

## Requirements

- 7+ years in analytics engineering or BI development with production Power BI experience
- Advanced Power BI proficiency including semantic models and DAX
- Microsoft Fabric integration or SSAS/AAS experience
- Strong analytical SQL capabilities
- Demonstrated ability to work with finance and trading professionals
- Bachelor's degree in quantitative/technical field or equivalent

## Preferred Qualifications

- Commodity trading or financial risk reporting background
- Power BI certification (PL-300 or PL-400)
- DAX optimization expertise
- Fabric Lakehouse/Warehouse experience
