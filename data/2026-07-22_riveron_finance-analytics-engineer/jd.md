Senior Associate - Finance Analytics Engineer

About Role

The Finance Analytics Engineer will be a hands-on contributor responsible for delivering finance-focused analytics solutions that combine strong data engineering capabilities with solid financial understanding. This role involves working closely with accounting and business data, building scalable data solutions, and translating data into clear, actionable insights for stakeholders.

Who You Are

5+ years of experience in analytics, BI, or data-focused roles
Strong proficiency in SQL and experience with Alteryx, Python, or similar tools
Comfortable working across data engineering and analytics use cases, with exposure to BI
Familiarity with financial data concepts (e.g., trial balances, P&L, revenue data) and the ability to translate them into analytical solutions
Experience supporting finance, accounting, or business teams is strongly preferred
Ability to clearly explain insights and findings to non-technical stakeholders
You have a passion for developing and maintaining client relationships
You get the job done and have fun doing it
You communicate skilfully with a variety of audiences and can create compelling stories from data
You thrive in an ever-changing, dynamic work environment
You readily identify problems and instinctively look for solutions
You enjoy participating in internal and external company initiatives such as community service, training, recruiting, and firm events

What You Will Do

Extract, transform, and integrate financial and operational data from multiple sources
Deliver end-to-end analytics and reporting solutions, including KPI definition, backend data preparation, and visualization dashboards
Design, build, and maintain automated and reusable data pipelines to support analytics and reporting
Write and optimize complex SQL queries to support analytical use cases
Use data preparation and automation tools (e.g., Alteryx) to streamline transformations
Work with enterprise data platforms including Microsoft Fabric, Azure, and Snowflake
Collaborate with business and functional stakeholders to understand reporting needs and translate them into data solutions
Apply financial and business context to ensure analytics outputs are accurate, consistent, and meaningful
Communicate insights, trends, and drivers clearly through dashboards and stakeholder discussions

About Riveron:

At Riveron, we partner with clients—from global multinationals to high-growth private entities—to solve complex finance challenges, guided by our DELTA values: Drive, Excellence, Leadership, Teamwork, and Accountability. Our entrepreneurial culture thrives on collaboration, diverse perspectives, and delivering exceptional outcomes.

Full time roles are eligible for a full range of benefits including medical, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) with company match, and PTO.

Artificial intelligence (AI) tools are used to support the hiring process in screening, assessing, and/or selecting applicants for this position. These tools assist our recruitment team but do not replace human judgment. Final hiring decisions are ultimately made by humans.
