Senior Analytics Engineer Position - Super Technologies

Company: Super Technologies
Job Title: Senior Analytics Engineer
Location: Croatia
Salary: Not specified

Key Responsibilities
The role centers on building analytical foundations. Candidates will "Design and build curated analytical datasets, metric implementations, and semantic models" while working with data warehouse modeling. Primary duties include partnering with stakeholders to translate business needs into reliable data structures, developing Tableau dashboards, and enhancing data quality practices.

Additional responsibilities involve refactoring ad-hoc reporting logic into reusable models, applying engineering rigor to analytics workflows, and fostering self-service data exploration capabilities.

Required Qualifications
Essential skills include strong SQL proficiency and hands-on experience with modern data warehouses (Snowflake preferred). Candidates should demonstrate solid knowledge of dimensional modeling, fact/dimension design, and reporting tools like Tableau.

The ideal candidate exhibits a "production mindset: care for reliability, maintainability, documentation, and operational ownership." Strong collaboration, ownership, and communication abilities are critical, particularly in ambiguous situations requiring cross-functional partnerships.

Preferred Qualifications
Valuable additions include experience with orchestration tools like Airflow, data governance platforms such as DataHub, and backgrounds in experimentation-driven or high-growth environments.

Benefits
Super Technologies offers medical insurance, open annual leave, employee assistance programs, and training development opportunities.
