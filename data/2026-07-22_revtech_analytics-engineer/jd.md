Analytics Engineer | London/Hybrid | £85,000 - £90,000

Fast-growing InsurTech rebuilding its entire data platform following the launch of a brand-new product, creating an opportunity for an Analytics Engineer to shape the future of its data function from the ground up.

This is a hands-on role spanning both Analytics Engineering and Data Engineering, where you'll build data models, reporting layers and pipelines while helping design the long-term data architecture.

You'll work closely with stakeholders across the business, translating requirements into scalable reporting and analytics solutions, while playing a key role in migrating from a legacy platform to a modern data stack.

It's an opportunity to join at a genuine build stage, taking ownership of data rather than maintaining someone else's environment.

What do you need?

Experience as an Analytics Engineer or Data Engineer

Strong SQL, dbt and Python experience

Knowledge of data modelling, data warehousing and ETL/ELT pipelines

Experience working with modern cloud data platforms (Azure preferred)

Strong stakeholder management and business-facing communication skills

A builder mindset, comfortable working in fast-moving, evolving environments

Desirable:

Azure

Prefect, Airflow or Dagster

Metabase or Tableau

Experience within InsurTech, FinTech or another regulated industry

Please apply if you'd like to learn more!
