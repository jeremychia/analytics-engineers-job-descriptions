# Senior Data Analytics Engineer

**Company:** Eunice Energy Group
**Job Title:** Senior Data Analytics Engineer
**Location:** Marousi, Attiki, Greece
**Seniority Level:** Mid-Senior level
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Job Function:** Information Technology
**Industry:** Renewable Energy Power Generation

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain analytical datasets and data products for Trading, Forecasting, Operations, and Portfolio Management
- Design core analytical assets: Asset Master, Portfolio Position, Forecast vs Actual, Exposure Analysis, Revenue Attribution, Settlement Analysis, and Portfolio KPIs
- Develop curated, business-ready data layers within AWS lakehouse
- Write SQL and Python transformations (dbt) that standardize business logic, KPIs, and analytical calculations
- Support Grafana dashboards and analytical reporting workflows
- Partner with Trading, Forecasting, Operations, and Commercial teams to translate business requirements into analytical solutions
- Drive data quality, governance, and KPI standardization

## Requirements

- 4+ years in Analytics Engineering, BI, Data Analytics, or Data Science
- Advanced SQL and strong Python
- Solid data modeling and analytics engineering; dbt experience strongly preferred
- Experience with PostgreSQL, Athena, or similar analytical databases
- Grafana, Power BI, or Tableau
- AWS analytics (Athena, S3, Glue) and Git (noted as a plus)
- Energy, Utilities, Trading, or Financial Services background strongly preferred
- Bachelor's in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, Economics, or related field; Master's a plus

**Ideal Profile:** A communicator bridging business and technical domains, thriving in cross-functional environments and converting complex business processes into reusable analytical solutions.
