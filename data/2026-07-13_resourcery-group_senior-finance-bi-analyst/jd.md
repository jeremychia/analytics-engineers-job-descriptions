## Overview

Resourcery Group seeks a Senior Finance Business Intelligence Analyst for an international business based in Liverpool. This hybrid role focuses on transforming "the way the business uses data to drive performance" through Power BI reporting solutions.

## Key Responsibilities

**Power BI Development & Data Modelling**
Design and maintain robust Power BI models, dashboards, and datasets. Build advanced DAX measures for margin analysis, KPIs, trends, and variance reporting while ensuring scalable, high-performance systems with strong governance.

**Management Reporting & Analysis**
Enhance finance processes through automation and reduce spreadsheet reliance. Support budgeting and forecasting with scalable models.

**Multi-System Reporting**
Collaborate with BI teams to integrate financial and operational data. Define standardized financial metrics across all reporting.

**Stakeholder Engagement**
Partner with finance and operational leaders to translate business needs into BI solutions and guide non-finance users.

## Key Requirements

- Strong end-to-end Power BI reports and dashboards experience
- Advanced knowledge of Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel, and SSRS
- Microsoft Fabric awareness understanding
- Solid SQL skills for data extraction and transformation
- Proven ability converting business requirements into reporting solutions
- Experience improving manual/Excel-based processes
- Cross-functional collaboration abilities
- Attention to data accuracy and quality
- Clear documentation and communication skills
- Time management and independent work capability
- Discretion handling confidential data

## Benefits

- Company bonus
- Enhanced pension
- Private health cover
- Hybrid and flexible working
