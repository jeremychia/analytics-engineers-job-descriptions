Senior Analyst, Data & Analytics - Jetstar

Company: Jetstar Airways Pty Ltd (Qantas Group)
Location: Docklands, Melbourne (head office); hybrid model with 3 days onsite
Employment Type: Full-time and Permanent
Application Deadline: July 29

Company Description
Jetstar operates around 3,000 flights to nearly 40 destinations across Australia, New Zealand, Asia and the Pacific with a fleet of more than 80 aircraft. The company emphasizes that "Our people are the real jet stars!"

Responsibilities
- Design and build gold-layer data assets and semantic features that power self-service business reporting.
- Deliver advanced analyses and insights that drive product, growth, and commercial strategy decisions.
- Design and deploy next-generation BI and analytics tools that give Commercial teams direct, confident self-serve access to data.
- Translate complex data into decision-ready outputs for senior leadership.
- Partner with stakeholders to define key metrics and ensure alignment across teams.
- Collaborate with BI, Data Engineering, and Product to maintain robust pipelines and models.
- Communicate technical issues, trade-offs, and opportunities clearly to business teams.
- Peer-review and provide feedback on the work of other analysts to maintain high standards.
- Implement version control practices and monitor for pipeline or data issues.
- Document workflows, ensure metric consistency, and promote best data governance practices.
- Identify and communicate strategic opportunities where data can unlock business value.

Required Qualifications
- 3-5 years in data analytics, data engineering, BI, or related roles.
- Advanced SQL and Python skills and strong experience with data modelling.
- Strong knowledge of cloud data warehouse architecture (Snowflake experience highly regarded).
- Hands-on experience with ETL tools (e.g. Fivetran, dbt).
- Strong commercial acumen to contextualise findings.
- Demonstrated experience designing and deploying production-grade agentic AI workflows.
- Familiarity with version control and collaborative workflows (e.g., Git).
- Ability to mentor and review work of more junior team members.
- Proven ability to deliver insights that shape strategy and performance.

Key Teams/Functions Referenced: Commercial, Central Data Engineering, BI, Product
