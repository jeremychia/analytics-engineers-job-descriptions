Analytics Engineer (3518) - Job Description

Position: Analytics Engineer
Location: New South Wales, Australia
Company: Smartgroup

About the Role:
Smartgroup seeks an experienced Analytics Engineer to join their Enterprise Data team, focusing on building scalable data products using modern technologies. The role bridges data engineering, analytics and stakeholder engagement.

Primary Responsibilities:
- Design and build scalable data products for reports and dashboards
- Develop analytics-ready datasets using data modeling techniques
- Work with Databricks, SQL, and Python for data solutions
- Partner with business and technical stakeholders on requirements
- Maintain data quality and documentation standards

Required Qualifications:
- 3+ years as an Analytics Engineer or similar role
- Hands-on Databricks/Snowflake experience with medallion architecture
- Advanced SQL and Python programming skills
- Ability to interpret business requirements into data models
- Strong communication and collaboration abilities

Benefits Offered:
Additional leave, free weekly lunch, up to 20 weeks paid parental leave, volunteer days, retail discounts, salary packaging access, flu vaccines, and employee share plans.

Application Requirements:
Background checks and police clearance required. Applicants must have unlimited Australian work rights. No agencies.
