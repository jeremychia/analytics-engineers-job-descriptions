# Senior Data Analytics and Visualization Engineer - Job Description

**Company:** EPAM

**Job Title:** Senior Data Analytics and Visualization Engineer

**Location:** Hybrid in Latvia

**Department:** Data Analytics and Visualization

**Salary:** Not specified

## Responsibilities

The role involves building "advanced-level dashboards and reports" and collaborating with product owners on requirements and demonstrations. Key duties include designing data models, preparing and profiling data, analyzing business problems, managing security, and participating in architecture and testing processes.

## Requirements

The position requires "5+ years of practical experience" with reporting tools like Power BI or Tableau, plus Python proficiency. Candidates need advanced SQL knowledge with relational databases and understanding of data architecture concepts including data warehouses and data lakes.

Additional requirements include hands-on cloud platform experience (AWS, Azure, GCP), data products testing expertise, agile development familiarity, CI/CD knowledge, and "Upper-Intermediate and higher" English proficiency.
