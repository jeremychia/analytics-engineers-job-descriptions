Data Analytics Engineer Job Description

Position: Data Analytics Engineer
Location: Seoul, Korea

Company Overview:
MarqVision is a brand protection solution company founded in 2020 by Harvard Law graduates. The company helps global brands identify and remove counterfeits and pirated content from over 1,500 platforms across 118 countries. Backed by Y Combinator and major investors, they recently completed a Series B funding round.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain scalable data pipelines supporting service growth
- Address data consistency issues across RDB and NoSQL sources
- Design data marts for company metrics and automated ad-hoc analysis
- Lead initial architecture design for new analytics platform implementation

Required Qualifications:
- 3 years or more of practical experience as a Data Engineer or Analytics Engineer
- Experience with Airflow or similar workflow orchestration tools
- Deep understanding of SQL (PostgreSQL, Snowflake, BigQuery, etc.)
- Data warehouse layer design experience
- Query optimization in large-scale database environments
- Experience resolving data consistency issues across sources

Preferred Qualifications:
- Snowflake and dbt modeling experience
- BI platform collaboration and operations
- Unstructured data handling
- CI/CD and Git proficiency
