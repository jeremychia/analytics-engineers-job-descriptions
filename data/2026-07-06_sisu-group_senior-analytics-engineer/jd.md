Company: Sisu Group
Job Title: Senior Analytics Engineer
Location: Tallinn (Revenues department)

Key Responsibilities
The role focuses on creating analytical solutions and maintaining data infrastructure. Candidates will "Design and build clear, compelling visualizations, dashboards, and reports" and take "ownership of our data ecosystem by building and maintaining robust data warehouse solutions."

Additional duties include configuring data integrations, implementing validation processes, and collaborating with product teams on data structure enhancements.

Required Qualifications
The posting seeks candidates with "over 3 years of hands-on work experience" in data analysis and engineering roles. Essential technical skills include:
- Expert-level SQL with performance optimization knowledge
- Dimensional data modeling and analytical dataset design
- BI tools proficiency (Power BI or Tableau)
- Pipeline orchestration experience (Airflow, Dataform, DBT)
- Basic Python for integrations and scripting
- Excellent English communication abilities

About the Company
Sisu Group is described as "a fast-growing iGaming startup" with 170 employees across Tallinn and Malaga offices. The company offers "competitive salaries with stock options" and emphasizes collaborative, non-corporate culture with stock ownership for team members.
