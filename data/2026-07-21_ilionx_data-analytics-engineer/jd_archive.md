# Data Analytics Engineer

**Company:** ilionx
**Job Title:** Data Analytics Engineer
**Location:** Zwolle, Overijssel, Netherlands
**Salary:** €3,500–€6,500/month (40 hours)
**Employment Type:** Full-time, permanent contract

## Role Description

As a Data Analytics Engineer, you'll design and implement scalable analytics solutions, acting as a trusted advisor who translates business challenges into robust data architectures. You'll work within multidisciplinary teams either at ilionx or embedded with clients across healthcare, government, retail, and finance sectors.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design data models and analytics solutions using Azure ecosystem tools
- Work with platforms including Fabric, Databricks, Synapse, Data Factory, and Data Lake
- Create performance-optimized visualizations using Power BI and Tabular Editor
- Establish data quality standards and best practices across teams
- Lead analytics team development and knowledge sharing initiatives
- Implement and research generative AI applications for innovation

## Requirements

- HBO or WO degree
- Minimum 3 years as BI/Data Specialist
- Proficiency in data analysis, modeling, and translating business needs
- Experience with Azure Data Services (Power BI, Fabric, Data Factory, Synapse, Data Lake)
- Knowledge of DAX, M-query, and SQL
- Strong Dutch communication skills (written and verbal)

## Benefits

- Competitive salary with performance bonuses
- Comprehensive benefits package including lease car allowance
- Flexible work arrangements
- Career development and leadership pathway opportunities
- Focus on AI-driven innovation
