Analytics Engineer

Voyado seeks an Analytics Engineer to develop the data infrastructure supporting analytics and AI features used by thousands of retailers. The role focuses on creating reliable, governed datasets and certified metrics as shared product assets.

Key Responsibilities
• Build governed data products with clear schemas, quality tests, lineage, and ownership
• Write SQL to model, transform, and validate structured data
• Establish and maintain semantic definitions and certified metrics
• Collaborate with domain teams to enhance data quality
• Create documentation and standards ensuring analytics reliability
• Support embedded BI and future AI-powered analytics capabilities

Required Qualifications
• Strong SQL proficiency for exploration, transformation, validation
• Experience with dbt or equivalent data transformation tools
• Background building data products, semantic layers, or certified metrics
• Understanding of lineage, data contracts, canonical entities, quality testing
• Familiarity with modern platforms like Databricks, Snowflake, or BigQuery

Preferred: Retail tech, e-commerce, or lakehouse platform experience
