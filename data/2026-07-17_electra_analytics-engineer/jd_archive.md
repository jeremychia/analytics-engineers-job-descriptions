Analytics Engineer - Electra Job Posting

Position: Analytics Engineer
Company: Electra
Location: Paris, France
Work Arrangement: Hybrid (2 days remote per week)
Employment Type: Full-time
Department: Product & Tech

Company Overview

Electra, founded in 2021, operates a network of fast-charging stations across Europe. The organization has "deployed +750 stations and 4,000+ fast-charging points" and grown to over 280 employees across multiple European cities.

Key Responsibilities

The role involves several core functions:

- Design and maintain data models using dbt across business domains
- Extend governance of metric definitions and semantic layers
- Shape data infrastructure to support LLM-powered analytics tools
- Onboard new data sources like SAP and electricity costs into structured models
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and anomaly detection systems
- Contribute to orchestration and data ingestion infrastructure

Required Qualifications

Essential technical skills include proficiency in SQL, dbt, data modeling, and experience with cloud data warehouses like Snowflake. The candidate should demonstrate commitment to "metric consistency and clear definitions" and emphasize data quality through testing and monitoring.

Preferred Experience

Nice-to-have skills encompass data observability tools, orchestration platforms (Dagster), BI tools, LLM-powered analytics, and AWS infrastructure knowledge.

Compensation & Benefits

The package includes:
- Attractive fixed and variable compensation (quarterly payments)
- 25 paid vacation days plus 12 RTT days
- Health insurance (50% covered), meal vouchers, mental health support
- Quarterly company events and sustainable mobility package
