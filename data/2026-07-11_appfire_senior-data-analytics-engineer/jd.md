Senior Data Analytics Engineer - Data Insights

Company: Appfire
Job Title: Senior Data Analytics Engineer - Data Insights
Location: Spain (fully remote within Spain, with optional Bilbao office access)
Salary Range: €40,000 – €70,000+ gross per year

Key Responsibilities
The role involves constructing and activating "Appfire's company-wide data asset (Snowflake) from ground up data sources." Primary duties include:
- Collaborating with stakeholders to integrate and build data metrics in Snowflake
- Architecting reliable data models and pipelines using DBT and Snowflake
- Designing metrics and dimensions for self-service analytics
- Establishing best practices for metric and data model development

Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of relevant industry experience
- Expert SQL proficiency for data modeling and query optimization
- 3+ years working with Python and DBT
- Strong data warehousing knowledge
- Remote work competency with proven project management abilities
- Excellent communication skills for explaining "technical concepts to a non-technical audience"

Preferred Skills
- DBT implementation expertise
- Cloud platform experience (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- Git or BitBucket proficiency
- Semi-structured data format handling (JSON)
- BI tool development (3+ years)
- Marketing or ML analytics background

Benefits
Equity participation, 25 days annual leave, flexible work arrangements, private health insurance, €50/month remote work stipend, and professional development through Appfire University.
