# Data Analytics Engineer - IDW Porto

**Company:** IDW
**Job Title:** Data Analytics Engineer
**Location:** Porto, Portugal
**Employment Type:** Full-time (Hybrid model)
**Seniority Level:** Mid-Senior level

## Key Requirements

The position requires more than 3 years of experience in Data Analyst, Analytics Engineer or BI Developer roles with balanced analysis and ETL development focus. Advanced SQL proficiency, relational data modeling knowledge using Kimball methodology, and hands-on ETL/ELT tool experience (Stambia, Talend, Informatica, SSIS, Data Factory, or dbt) are essential. Minimum B2 French and fluent English are mandatory.

## Main Responsibilities

Candidates will collaborate with business areas to translate functional needs into data models and technical specifications. Core duties include designing optimized data models, developing performance dashboards, ensuring data quality, and creating ETL/ELT pipelines—primarily in Stambia—plus implementing automated data quality checks.

## Benefits Offered

The company provides market-compatible salary, health insurance, meal vouchers, continuous feedback, career development opportunities, and exclusive benefits in health, wellness, education, dining, culture and leisure alongside a talent referral program.
