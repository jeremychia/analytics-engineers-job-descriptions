BI Engineer Job Posting

Position: BI Engineer
Company: Fruition Group Ireland
Location: County Westmeath, Ireland
Employment Type: Permanent (Hybrid)
Seniority Level: Mid-Senior level

Key Responsibilities

The role involves designing and maintaining Power BI dashboards, converting business requirements into effective visualizations, and developing semantic data models. The engineer will create DAX calculations, monitor report performance, manage assets in Microsoft Fabric and Power BI Service, implement security measures, and support user acceptance testing and production releases.

Required Experience

Candidates need "minimum of 3 years' experience developing and supporting Power BI solutions" in commercial settings. Essential skills include Power BI Desktop, Power BI Service, DAX, Power Query, SQL proficiency, and semantic modeling knowledge. Experience translating business requirements and managing multiple priorities is required.

Desirable Skills

Experience with Microsoft Fabric, financial services environments, cloud analytics platforms, UX/UI principles, reporting governance, and Power BI capacity management are advantageous.

Education

A third-level qualification in Computer Science, Data Analytics, or related discipline is expected. Microsoft or Power BI certifications are advantageous.

Work Arrangement

"Permanent position with standard Monday-to-Friday working hours" with "office attendance required 4 days per week." The employer offers competitive salary, professional development opportunities, and exposure to large-scale analytics initiatives.
