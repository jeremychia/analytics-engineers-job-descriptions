Data Analytics Engineer - Shenzhen at CASETiFY

Location: Shenzhen
Company: CASETiFY

Purpose of Position
The Data Analytics Engineer will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining scalable analytics data solutions that support CASETiFY's business intelligence, KPI reporting, dashboarding, and insight generation across the organization.

Key Responsibilities
- Design and maintain analytics-ready datasets, transformation logic, and semantic models
- Work with BI, Data Engineering, and stakeholders to understand reporting requirements
- Develop and maintain curated data models, reusable metrics, and governed datasets that support management reporting, self-service analytics, and insight generation
- Develop reports and dashboards using Tableau or Power BI
- Define transformation logic and business rules for consistency
- Support KPI setup and metric governance
- Collaborate with Data Engineering on sustainable data pipelines
- Establish and maintain strong metadata, documentation, lineage, and semantic consistency for analytics datasets and reporting layers

Requirements
- Strong hands-on experience in analytics engineering, data transformation, and data modeling for BI and reporting use cases
- Solid SQL skills for building curated datasets
- Experience with KPI setup and metric standardization
- Good understanding of data warehousing concepts, dimensional modeling, and transformation workflows
- At least 3-6 years of relevant working experience in analytics engineering, BI development, data analytics, or related data roles
- BI tool proficiency (Tableau, Power BI)
- eCommerce/retail experience preferred
- Proficient English; Chinese is a plus
