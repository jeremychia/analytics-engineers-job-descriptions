Who are we?

Southern Water is building a modern, trusted data platform to support better decision-making, regulatory performance and customer outcomes. Our Business Intelligence teams play a critical role in turning complex data into clear, actionable insight across the organisation.

This is an exciting opportunity to join a newly formed BI Engineering team and help shape how business intelligence is delivered, governed and improved across Southern Water.

The role

As a Senior BI Engineer, you will help lead the day-to-day delivery of BI solutions within a domain-aligned agile squad.

This role would suit an experienced BI Developer or BI Engineer who is ready to take the next step into technical coordination, mentoring and delivery leadership. You do not need to have done every aspect of the role before, but you will need strong Power BI and semantic modelling experience, good SQL skills, strong communication, and the confidence to support other BI developers.

You will act as a key link between business stakeholders, Product Owners, Data Modellers, Data Engineers and Platform teams — helping translate business needs into practical, scalable and well-governed BI solutions.

The role includes hands-on BI development, technical guidance, squad-level coordination and mentoring. It is ideal for someone who enjoys building high-quality BI solutions, but also wants to help shape standards, improve ways of working and support the team to deliver effectively.

What you'll be doing

Designing, building and maintaining Power BI reports, datasets and semantic models
Helping translate business requirements into clear, practical BI solutions
Supporting day-to-day BI engineering delivery within a domain-aligned squad
Coordinating work across BI Engineers, Data Modellers and Data Engineers
Supporting prioritisation, backlog refinement and delivery planning
Acting as a bridge between business stakeholders and technical delivery teams
Applying good practice in Power BI performance, usability, security and access control
Documenting report logic, data sources, measures, definitions and model design
Supporting the development of certified metrics and a single version of the truth
Reviewing and improving existing BI assets, models and reporting processes
Using SQL and modern data platform tools to validate data and support report development
Supporting data quality, governance and compliance requirements within BI solutions
Mentoring and guiding BI Engineers, including sharing knowledge and reviewing work
Helping establish reusable BI standards, patterns and delivery processes
Contributing to agile ceremonies and continuous improvement across the BI community
What we're looking for

We are looking for someone with strong BI development experience who can combine technical capability with good communication, stakeholder engagement and team working.

You may already be working as a Senior BI Engineer, or you may be an experienced BI Developer / BI Engineer ready to step into a broader role with more ownership, coordination and mentoring.

Essential skills and experience

You'll need:

Strong experience developing Power BI reports, datasets and semantic models
Good understanding of DAX, Power Query and Power BI performance considerations
Strong SQL skills and experience working with relational data sources
Understanding of data modelling concepts, including dimensional or semantic modelling
Ability to gather, document and translate business requirements into reporting solutions
Good communication skills, including the ability to work with non-technical stakeholders
Experience working as part of a technical delivery team
Ability to support, mentor or guide other BI developers
Awareness of data quality, governance, security and access control principles
A proactive approach to problem solving, documentation and continuous improvement
Desirable skills and experience

It would be helpful if you also have experience with:

Databricks, Python or PySpark
Azure Data Lake, Delta Lake or other modern cloud data platforms
Row-level security and access controls in Power BI
Deployment, release management or monitoring of BI solutions
Agile delivery methods and tools such as Jira or Confluence
Metadata, lineage, data cataloguing or governance tooling
Domain-oriented, product-based or data mesh-style teams
Previous formal line management or team leadership experience
