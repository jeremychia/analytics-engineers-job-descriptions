# Senior Analytics Engineer

**Company:** Avalanche Studios Group
**Job Title:** Senior Analytics Engineer
**Location:** Stockholm (Södermalm office)
**Salary:** Not stated

## Responsibilities

The role involves developing processes to extract, clean, and transform data from various sources for analysis. Key duties include collaborating with data scientists on their needs, designing data models covering both in-game and out-of-game experiences, and maintaining documentation like data dictionaries and schemas.

The position requires monitoring pipelines for high availability and performance and implementing quality checks. Code reviews and staying current with industry trends are also core responsibilities.

## Requirements

Candidates should have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or related field with 5+ years experience as an Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, Data Architect, or similar role.

Essential skills include strong SQL proficiency, experience with ETL/ELT tools (DBT preferred), and familiarity with cloud platforms like GCP. Infrastructure as Code experience is needed. The role demands problem-solving abilities and cross-functional collaboration.

## Work Arrangement

The hybrid model requires presence on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays at the office. No relocation assistance is available.
