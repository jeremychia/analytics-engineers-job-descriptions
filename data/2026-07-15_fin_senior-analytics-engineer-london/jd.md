Senior Analytics Engineer at Fin

Location: London, England

Salary: Not specified

Job Description Overview

Fin seeks a Senior Analytics Engineer to join their Analytics Engineering team. The role focuses on building data infrastructure and models that support business-wide decision-making.

Key Responsibilities

- Collaborate with data infrastructure teams to maintain and enhance the data warehouse and ELT systems
- Design and build data models and core assets within the data warehouse
- Partner with internal analyst teams to address their analytical needs
- Simplify and improve data collection and reporting processes to automate manual workflows
- Identify and resolve data quality issues across systems

Required Qualifications

- 3+ years as an Analytics Engineer
- Advanced SQL proficiency with emphasis on data modeling and query optimization
- Experience with modern data stack tools (dbt and Snowflake preferred)
- Growth mindset and willingness to learn
- Strong communication skills adapted for diverse audiences
- Sound business judgment and product instincts

Preferred Qualifications

- Apache Airflow or DAG framework experience
- Tableau, Looker, or similar BI platform experience
- Exposure to operational tools (Google Analytics, Salesforce, Segment, Zuora, Marketo)
- Python familiarity

Benefits Highlights

- Competitive salary and equity
- Daily lunches and snacks
- Unlimited access to Claude Code and AI tools
- Pension matching (up to 4%)
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance
- Flexible PTO and paid family leave
- Hybrid work (3 days/week in-office required)
