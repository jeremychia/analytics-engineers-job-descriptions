# Senior Analytics Engineer - TeamViewer Germany GmbH

**Company:** TeamViewer Germany GmbH

**Job Title:** Senior Analytics Engineer

**Location:** Porto, Portugal

**Remote Status:** Hybrid

**Employment Type:** Full-time

---

## Job Summary

The role involves helping TeamViewer optimize operations by owning standardized KPIs, building reliable data models, and implementing quality checks that inform decisions across business units.

## Key Responsibilities

- Maintain and enhance "data applications that optimize various business processes"
- Act as liaison between analytics platform and business consumers
- Guarantee data quality through testing, monitoring, and validation
- Design standardized business metrics and KPIs for performance analysis
- Optimize warehouse pipelines for datasets serving 2+ billion users globally
- Address data issues, participate in on-call support, and suggest improvements

## Required Qualifications

- Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related field
- 3-5 years professional experience in data/analytics roles
- Strong SQL proficiency with complex query writing
- Python knowledge for data processing
- Project delivery experience in data handling
- Understanding of software engineering practices (testing, modularity, reviews)
- Problem-solving abilities and collaborative mindset
- "Ability to communicate effectively with non-technical stakeholders"
- English fluency

## Preferred Skills

- dbt or similar transformation framework experience
- Cloud data warehouse expertise (BigQuery, Redshift)
- BI tool familiarity (Looker, Tableau)
- Git version control experience
