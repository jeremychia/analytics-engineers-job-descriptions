Role Purpose
As a Senior Data & Analytics Engineer within HEINEKEN's D&A Product Organisation, the role focuses on realising the Smart Brewery ambition. The position involves embedding within Supply Chain product squads (Packaging, Brewing, or Utilities) as a senior expert — bringing depth, structure, and technical leadership.

Key aspects include combining strong business analysis skills, advanced BI development expertise, and mature product thinking to translate operational needs into analytical products.

Key Responsibilities

1. Product Discovery & Requirements
- Translate complex business needs into analytical and functional designs
- Contribute to the refinement of global, reusable use cases
- Assess feasibility and data readiness

2. Stakeholder Engagement
- Communicate (complex) analytical concepts, modelling decisions, and data logic
- Partner with SMEs and brewery teams

3. Data & Analytics Enablement
- Design and build high-quality, scalable dashboards, semantic models, and analytical features
- Use Power BI, Grafana, or related tools
- Create advanced DAX measures, optimised Power Query transformations

4. Product Delivery & Value Realisation
- Provide senior level guidance throughout sprints
- Monitor adoption and value realisation

5. Supporting Agile Ways of Working
- Break down complex (analytical) requirements into clear, testable backlog items
- Mentor team members

6. Change & Adoption
- Develop high-quality documentation
- Support deployment activities

Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, Data/Analytics
- Significant experience in Business Analysis, BI Development, or Data Engineering
- Demonstrated experience delivering complex BI/analytics solutions in Agile environments

Essential Skills & Experience
- Advanced BI development expertise (Power BI, Grafana) including semantic modelling, DAX, Power Query
- Strong ability to translate business questions into analytical logic
- Ability to interpret and work with data structures and pipeline outputs
- Strong SQL skills, analytical validation skills
- Proven experience designing intuitive, scalable analytical models
- Excellent communication skills with the ability to influence and guide
- Experience working in global, cross-functional and multicultural product teams
- Fluent in English (written & spoken)

Desirable Skills
- Experience in manufacturing, FMCG, or industrial analytics (MES, SCADA, sensors)
- Conceptual understanding of Azure analytics tools (ADF, Synapse, Databricks, Azure SQL)
- Experience with KPI frameworks, operational monitoring, or continuous improvement methodologies
