## Key Responsibilities

The role focuses on building and scaling a modern data platform. Core duties include designing orchestration workflows using Airflow, developing models with dbt, implementing data transformations, building dimensional models and KPI logic, configuring BigQuery datasets, and establishing data quality and governance controls. The position bridges data engineering, analytics modeling, and governance.

## Required Qualifications

Candidates should demonstrate strong SQL proficiency and experience with analytical platforms like BigQuery. Hands-on dbt expertise (models, tests, macros, documentation) and Airflow orchestration experience are essential. Strong Python skills for data processing and pipeline automation are required, along with dimensional modeling knowledge and production-level data governance implementation experience. Git workflows and CI/CD familiarity are also necessary.

## Preferred Experience
