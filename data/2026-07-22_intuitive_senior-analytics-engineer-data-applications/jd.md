Senior Analytics Engineer – Data Applications

Location: Bengaluru, INDIA, India
Job ID: JOB215564
Department: Information Technology & Systems
Work Arrangement: Not Remote

Company Description
It started with a simple idea: what if surgery could be less invasive and recovery less painful? Intuitive is a global leader in robotic-assisted surgery, known for technologies like the da Vinci surgical system and Ion.

Primary Function
The role seeks a professional to design and develop interactive, data-driven applications using open-source Python frameworks integrated with modern data platforms like Snowflake and dbt. The position combines analytics engineering expertise with full-stack Python development.

Essential Responsibilities

Data Engineering & Analytics:
- Design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines using Python, dbt, Airflow, and/or Spark
- Develop curated datasets with well-defined metrics
- Ensure data quality through testing frameworks and monitoring

Application Development:
- Design and develop user-centric, interactive web applications using open-source frameworks such as Plotly Dash, Panel, or FastAPI
- Build modular, reusable components for visualizations and workflows
- Deliver scalable full-stack Python solutions

Integration & Platform:
- Integrate applications with Snowflake, S3, Smartsheet, and REST APIs
- Support CI/CD pipelines, version control, and containerized deployments (Docker)

Collaboration:
- Partner with supply chain, operations, and analytics teams
- Mentor junior engineers and promote best practices

Requirements

Required Experience:
- 10+ years of overall experience and 5+ years of relevant experience in analytics engineering, data engineering, or full-stack development
- Strong SQL and data modeling proficiency
- Extensive experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake strongly preferred)
- Advanced Python skills with Dash, Panel, or similar frameworks
- RESTful API development using FastAPI or Flask
- dbt, Airflow, or similar orchestration tools
- Git, CI/CD pipelines, Docker experience
- Significant experience in supply chain, logistics, manufacturing, or operations analytics environments

Education:
- Minimum of a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related field

Preferred Qualifications:
- Internal data product and self-service analytics design experience
- Authentication and multi-user application deployment knowledge
- Tableau or Power BI familiarity
- Supply chain process knowledge
- Data governance and enterprise data management expertise
