Analytics Engineer, India - DoorDash India

Location: Hyderabad, India

Company Description
At DoorDash, our mission to empower local economies shapes how our team members move quickly, learn, and reiterate in order to make impactful decisions that display empathy for our range of users—from Dashers to merchant partners to consumers.

Role Description
The Analytics Engineering team builds internal data products enabling data-driven decisions across business units. The role focuses on building and scaling the data foundations that enable fast, reliable, and actionable insights in collaboration with data engineers, scientists, and business partners.

Key Responsibilities
- Collaborate with data scientists, data engineers, and business stakeholders to understand business needs
- Identify key business questions and problems to solve for, and generate insights
- Lead the development of data products and self-serve tools
- Build and maintain canonical datasets by developing high-volume, reliable ETL/ELT pipelines
- Design metrics and data visualizations with dashboarding tools like Tableau, Sigma, and Mode

Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of experience working in business intelligence, analytics engineering, data engineering
- Strong proficiency in SQL for data transformation, comfort in at least one functional/OOP language
- Expertise in creating compelling reporting and data visualization solutions using dashboarding tools
- Familiarity with database fundamentals and SQL performance tuning experience
- Experience in writing data quality checks to validate data integrity
- Excellent communication skills and experience working with technical and non-technical teams
