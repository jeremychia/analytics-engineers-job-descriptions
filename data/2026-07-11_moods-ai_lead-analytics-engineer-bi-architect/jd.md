Lead Analytics Engineer / BI Architect - Job Description

Company: Moods AI
Job Title: Lead Analytics Engineer / BI Architect
Location: Remote (Europe) or Hybrid (The Netherlands)
Employment Type: Part-time or Full-time (16-40 hours per week)
Seniority Level: Mid-Senior level
Industry: Hospitals and Health Care

Overview
Moods AI seeks an experienced professional to design and own the analytical foundation of their mental healthcare platform. The role focuses on building "the trusted data foundation that makes AI useful, explainable and safe in mental healthcare."

Key Responsibilities
The position involves designing enterprise semantic layers and establishing governance standards across the organization. Specific duties include:
- Designing and maintaining Microsoft Fabric and Power BI semantic models
- Building scalable ETL/ELT pipelines and data integrations
- Developing KPI frameworks and executive dashboards
- Establishing data governance and quality standards
- Integrating healthcare systems, financial tools, and external APIs
- Enabling AI agents with business context

Requirements

Essential Experience (5+ years):
- Business Intelligence, Analytics Engineering, or BI Architecture
- Advanced SQL and semantic data modeling skills
- Deep Power BI expertise including DAX and governance
- ETL/ELT pipeline development using cloud platforms
- KPI framework design and enterprise reporting

Advantageous Skills:
- Microsoft Fabric architecture
- Python programming
- Healthcare data platforms
- LLM and RAG applications
- API and SaaS integrations

Compensation & Benefits
- Competitive salary (amount not specified)
- Flexible hours (16-40 weekly)
- Remote or hybrid options
- Modern Microsoft technology stack
- High ownership and direct influence on architecture
