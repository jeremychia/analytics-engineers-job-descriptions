## Key Responsibilities

**Own Marketing Data Modeling**
- Design, build, and maintain our core marketing data models — you know them by heart and can answer any question with data within minutes

- Educate analysts, engineers, marketers, and stakeholders on marketing data usage and meaning

**Own the Marketing Tech Data Stack**
- Take full ownership of the stability of our marketing data pipelines — be the first to respond to incidents and drive resolution
- Maintain expertise across marketing tech stack including ad platforms, Airbyte, Zoho CRM, GTM, and web analytics
- Partner with marketers to automate and improve marketing processes using data

**Drive Cross-Team Collaboration**
- Work with data analysts, engineers, software engineers, and marketing teams
- Lead and ship large cross-functional projects with clarity and alignment

**Mentor Junior Talent**
- Mentor junior data analysts and engineers
- Actively reduce your bus factor: document your work, teach what you know
