# Analytics Engineer till Borgo

**Company:** Borgo (recruiting via Amendo)
**Location:** Stockholm
**Salary:** Not specified

## Key Responsibilities

The role focuses on making data accessible and valuable. Primary duties include:

- Äga och utveckla dbt-lagret (modellering, tester, dokumentation) [managing dbt layer with modeling, testing, documentation]
- Building analytical data models and semantic layers
- Creating dashboards and reports using Power BI
- Collaborating with stakeholders to translate needs into solutions
- Ensuring clear definitions and high data quality
- Supporting organizational adoption of data-driven practices

## Required Qualifications

Candidates should demonstrate:

- Strong experience with dbt, SQL, and data modeling
- Experience using BI tools, preferably Power BI
- History working closely with business stakeholders
- Erfarenhet av att ta data från modell till insikt [ability to translate data models into actionable insights]

## Preferred Qualifications

- Snowflake or modern data platform experience
- Banking/finance or regulated environment background
- Data governance expertise
- Track record driving data-driven organizational transformation
