## Job Description

MoonPay seeks an experienced data analytics engineer to support accounting and financial planning functions. The role focuses on building robust data infrastructure and models for cryptocurrency and fiat payment reconciliation.

### Key Responsibilities

- Construct data models downstream of backend systems and external data sources for reconciliation and reporting
- Develop "The Ledger" system tracking crypto and fiat movements from wallet and exchange data
- Design MoonPay-wide reconciliation approaches for cash, crypto, and payments using internal and external sources
- Perform analysis and build solutions for product flows, inventory tracking, and cost basis reporting
- Investigate issues related to crypto delivery timing and payment reconciliation with finance and engineering teams
- Partner with accounting to create custom reporting and data tables

### Required Qualifications

- Advanced SQL proficiency and data modeling expertise
- Vision for architecting robust data models
- Adaptability and willingness to learn new technologies in dynamic environments

### Preferred Qualifications

- Finance team collaboration experience
- Interest in accounting, audits, or financial reporting
- Strong cross-functional communication skills with engineers and executives

The company emphasizes their core values: "Be Hungry, Level Up, Own It, Crypto Curious, Kaizen."
