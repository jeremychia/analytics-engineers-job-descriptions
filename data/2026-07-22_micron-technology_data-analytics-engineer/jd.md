Data Analytics Engineer - Micron Technology

Position: Data Analytics Engineer
Company: Micron Technology
Location: Singapore, Singapore
Seniority Level: Entry level
Employment Type: Full-time

Job Description
Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life for all.

The role focuses on using AI to accelerate projects with three core responsibility areas:

IMPLEMENT AND MAINTAIN DATA DRIVEN DECISION MAKING PROCESSES:
- Promote a data driven culture
- Provide quality data solutions that create business value
- Create metrics to gauge projects, data quality, and success
- Develop KPIs and metrics correlating to on time delivery and inventory

COLLECT, ORGANIZE, AUTOMATE AND PREPARE DATA FOR ANALYSIS:
- Work with various volumes of data from multiple disparate sources
- Perform data analysis and mining to help generate solutions
- Manage and optimize data discovery and cleansing

CREATE DATA ANALYTICS SOLUTIONS:
- Translate business problems into projects and solutions
- Conduct and participate in analysis, design, statistical/model algorithm review
- Write, code, test, and analyze data solutions, applications and automation

Requirements

Education:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Engineering, Statistics, or Engineering

Experience:
- 2 years experience of Industry Experience preferred
- Strong initiative to find AI Solutions on resolving issues

Skills:
- Strong analytical, mathematical, problem solving skills
- Proficiency in Claude Code, Advance Co-Pilot, Gemini, Java, Python, NumPy, Perl, C/C++, SQL
- Familiar with UNIX/Linux, subversion, agile development methods
- Semiconductor manufacturing environment a plus
