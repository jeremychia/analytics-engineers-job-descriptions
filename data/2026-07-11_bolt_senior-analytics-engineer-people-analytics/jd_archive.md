About the role
As a Senior Analytics Engineer in the People Analytics team, you will be much more than a data expert — you will actively shape how Bolt understands, plans, and develops its workforce at scale. You will own the People Analytics layer of Bolt's centrally managed data platform, building and maintaining the data infrastructure that powers people insights across the company: from headcount planning and hiring to attrition, absence, and compensation analytics.

A central part of this role is building the data infrastructure that makes conversational analytics possible. We are actively developing a people data plugin for AI tools, enabling analysts and business stakeholders to query people data in natural language. For that to work, the underlying data needs to be exceptionally clean, well-documented, semantically consistent, and structured with AI consumption in mind. You will own that foundation.

Main tasks and responsibilities
- Work closely with people analysts, people business partners, finance, and adjacent stakeholders to prioritise and align the analytics engineering roadmap, ensuring solutions meet both reporting and AI-readiness requirements.
- Build, expand, and maintain reusable data models and metrics in source of truth semantic layer (currently in dbt) covering the full employee lifecycle — headcount, hiring funnel, attrition, absence, and compensation — modelled with the consistency and documentation that LLM-based tools require to reason over data reliably. Implement data quality monitoring and alerting so issues are caught before they surface in dashboards or AI tools.
- Set up and manage the architecture, documentation, and key data transformations for people data source systems within our Databricks and S3-based data lake; identify and resolve gaps where data is missing, unreliable, or structurally unusable.
- Own and evolve the BI tool (currently Looker) semantic layer (ML models, explores, derived tables) as the single source of truth for people metrics — and as a key component of our conversational analytics setup.
- Co-build and extend our people data plugin for AI tools such as Claude and Cursor; work with the analytics engineering function and data platform team to establish standards and best practices for AI-ready data — including naming conventions, metric glossaries, lineage documentation, and data catalogue maintenance.
- Follow system design and architectural best practices and closely collaborate with Data Engineering, People Tech, and Internal Products to ensure data integrity and efficiency across all processes.

About you
