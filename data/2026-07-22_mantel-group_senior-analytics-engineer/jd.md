Senior Analytics Engineer - Mantel

Job Title: Senior Analytics Engineer
Location: Perth, Sydney
Remote Status: Hybrid
Employment Type: Full-time
Department: Data & AI

Company Description
Mantel is an Australian-owned, IT Professional Services consultancy, focused on emerging technology that helps clients leverage AI, Digital, Cloud, Data & Security technology.

Key Responsibilities
- Deliver Mantel's core analytics offerings and provide technical guidance on client projects
- Create and maintain scalable data pipelines, models, dashboards and reports
- Work across a range of modern data tools following best DataOps practices
- Provide clients with guidance and recommendations on the right data tools
- Lead small teams and work on complex projects independently or collaboratively
- Help identify potential opportunities within projects and client relationships
- Mentor and train junior team members
- Participate in community events promoting Mantel's capabilities

Requirements
- Previous experience in a hands-on data analytics role, BI or related role in a consulting organisation
- Demonstrated hands-on data experience across BI, data warehousing, data modelling, modern cloud platforms
- Proficiency in SQL, Python and tools like dbt, Snowflake, Databricks, Redshift, Azure Data Factory, Power BI, Qlik, Looker
- Previous application of DataOps tools and best practices
- Ability to guide projects independently and mentor others
