## Key Responsibilities

The role bridges business and analytics functions. Professionals in this position help uncover business needs and how to support them with data, reporting, and visualization. They write production-grade data pipelines and leverage modern tooling for data modeling, testing, and documentation.

## Requirements

Ideal candidates have:
- Passion for data and analytics
- Training in data analysis, science, or engineering with interest in data modeling
- Strong SQL query writing abilities and Python fundamentals
- Excellent communication skills
- Expertise in one or more analytics engineering areas

## What Won't Fit

The role is unsuitable for those who prefer purely academic approaches, avoid stakeholder communication, lack proactive tendencies, or dislike knowledge sharing through training and documentation.

## Benefits & Work Arrangement

Xebia offers free healthy lunches, mental/physical wellness benefits, generous tech budgets, sandbox cloud environments (GCP, AWS, Azure), monthly innovation time, €5,000 annual development budget, and mobility allowances. The modern Amsterdam office is dog-friendly.

Relocation assistance is not provided.
