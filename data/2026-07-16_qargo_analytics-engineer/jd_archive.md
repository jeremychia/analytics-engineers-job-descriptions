## Job Description Overview

Qargo seeks an Analytics Engineer to develop data models and dashboards for their Transport Management Platform. The role involves collaborating across departments to transform complex datasets into actionable insights.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Build and maintain data models and pipelines
- Create Lightdash dashboards for operational and financial insights
- Develop internal BI dashboards and account management reporting
- Support product analytics using Mixpanel
- Design billing and cost-analysis dashboards

**Required Qualifications:**
- Minimum 2 years experience in analytics or BI engineering
- Strong SQL and data modeling skills
- Proficiency with dashboarding tools (Lightdash, Superset, Tableau, Looker, or Power BI)
- Background in Computer Science, Data Engineering, or Statistics

**Offered Benefits:**
The company provides "real impact and ownership in a growing international scale-up," collaborative team culture, hybrid flexibility, learning opportunities, and "competitive salary and benefits package."

**Company Context:** Qargo, founded in 2020, operates as a SaaS platform with 182 employees across London, Ghent, and Chicago offices.
