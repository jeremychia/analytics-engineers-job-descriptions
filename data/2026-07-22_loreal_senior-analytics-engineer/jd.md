Senior Analytics Engineer – Data & Analytics

Location: Hyderabad (Telangana), Telangana
Employment Type: Permanent, Full-Time
Deadline: 25-Jun-2026

Company Description
For 115 years, L'Oréal, the world's leading beauty player, has devoted itself to one thing only: fulfilling the beauty aspirations of consumers around the world. L'Oréal operates in 150 countries with €42 billion in consolidated sales and maintains 37 global beauty brands across four divisions. The company acquired Aēsop in 2023 and currently employs over 2,000 tech professionals.

Key Responsibilities
- Architect and build the 'Transformation Layer' in BigQuery, turning raw data into high-quality, usable datasets
- Collaborate with Data Scientists and Analysts to create specialized datasets for predictive modelling
- Implement testing and documentation within the transformation pipeline to ensure data accuracy
- Translate business contexts into robust technical logic
- Design and develop logic that transforms raw BigQuery data into curated datasets

Required Qualifications
- Expert SQL: Complex query writing (CTEs, Window Functions)
- Data Modelling: Dimensional Modelling, Star Schemas expertise
- Previous experience with dbt (data build tool)
- Experience in developing high performant pipelines leveraging Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and BigQuery
- Proficiency in Git and version control best practices

Preferred Qualifications
- Python proficiency
- CI/CD pipelines and Terraform knowledge
- Experience with Power BI or Data Science model consumption
