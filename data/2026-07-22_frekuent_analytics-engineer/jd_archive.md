What You'll Do

At Frekuent, data isn't a support function, it's how we make decisions. As our Analytics Engineer, you'll sit at the intersection of data and business: turning questions from across the company into clear, reliable answers, and building the foundations that make every team smarter. You'll have a real impact from day one.

Be the data partner for Commercial, Operations Product, and Leadership teams understanding their questions and turning them into data deliverables that actually get used.

Design, build, and maintain dashboards and data visualizations that translate complex data into clear, actionable insights.

Help define and implement our data models and data warehouse layer.

Build and maintain ELT pipelines to support analytics needs across the company.

Set up monitoring, documentation, and quality checks to ensure data you publish is data people can trust.

Use AI tools to accelerate analysis, automate repetitive data tasks, and surface insights faster across the business.

Contribute to building a data-driven culture — helping teams ask better questions and interpret results with confidence.

Collaborate with the Data team to improve our modern data stack and shared tooling.

What We're Looking For

3+ years of hands-on experience in a data-related role (Data Analyst, Analytics Engineer, BI Analyst, or similar).

Strong SQL skills, capable of writing complex queries across multiple tables.

Comfortable using Python for data manipulation and analysis

Experience with data visualization tools such as Power BI, Looker, Tableau, or similar.

Experience with cloud data warehouses (e.g., BigQuery, Snowflake, Microsoft Fabric, Azure Synapse).

Ability to translate business questions into data requirements and results back into business language.

Familiarity with dbt or similar data modeling tools is a strong plus.

Comfortable working in a fast-moving startup environment — proactive, hands-on, and ready to take ownership.

Proficiency in English and conversational Spanish.
