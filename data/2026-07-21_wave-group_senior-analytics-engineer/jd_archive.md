# Senior Analytics Engineer

**Company:** Wave Group
**Job Title:** Senior Analytics Engineer
**Location:** London Area, United Kingdom (Shoreditch, 4 office days/week)
**Salary:** £80,000 - £100,000/year

## Responsibilities

The role involves designing the data foundations that power real-time operational decisions and constructing core data models for analytics and AI workflows. Key duties include owning data inputs for AI systems, implementing quality controls through testing and monitoring, and establishing analytics engineering best practices during company expansion.

## Requirements

**Essential qualifications:**
- 5+ years in analytics engineering, data engineering, or similar roles
- Strong SQL proficiency (BigQuery, PostgreSQL)
- Extensive dbt experience
- Data visualization tool competency
- Python skills for automation and data work
- Clear communication bridging technical and non-technical teams

**Desired experience:**
- Orchestration tools (Dagster/Airflow)
- Real-time pipeline development
- ML workflows and feature engineering familiarity

## Benefits & Work Arrangement

The compensation package features up to 170% equity with 70% guaranteed upon joining plus performance-based allocation. Additional benefits include private healthcare/dental, enhanced parental leave, office lunches, and visa sponsorship availability.
