Company: Niji

Job Description

Niji seeks an Analytics Engineer to design and maintain an Analytics Factory serving thousands of users. The role involves advising on data integration, analyzing existing flows, organizing data for dashboards, developing transformations, and ensuring data pipeline maintenance.

Key Responsibilities

- Advise on data integration and platform selection
- Analyze flows and identify optimization opportunities
- Organize and prepare data for dashboards
- Develop and document data transformations
- Maintain end-to-end data chains

Required Qualifications

- University degree or engineering school diploma (Bac +5 equivalent)
- Minimum 5 years professional analytics experience
- Strong SQL proficiency
- PowerBI expertise
- Proficiency in Python, R, or similar data analysis languages
- Organized, collaborative, and curious mindset

Environment

SQL, MSBI, Scala, Spark, Lakehouses

Benefits

- Impactful projects with major clients
- Multidisciplinary teams
- Professional development via Niji University
- Flexible work arrangements and generous benefits
- Commitment to diversity and accessibility
