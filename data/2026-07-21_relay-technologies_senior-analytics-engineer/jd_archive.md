# Senior Analytics Engineer

**Company:** Relay Technologies
**Job Title:** Senior Analytics Engineer
**Location:** London - Hybrid
**Employment Type:** FullTime

Relay is fundamentally reshaping how goods move in an online era. Backed by Europe's largest-ever logistics Series A ($35M), led by deep-tech investors Plural (whose portfolio spans fusion energy and space exploration), Relay is scaling faster than 99.98% of venture-backed startups. We're assembling the most talent-dense team the logistics industry has ever seen.

Relay's Mission is to free commerce from friction. Today, high delivery costs act as a hidden tax on e-commerce, quietly shaping what can be sold online and limiting who can participate. We envision a world where more goods move more freely between more people, making the online shopping experience seamless and accessible to everyone.

## The Opportunity

At Relay, we rely on data to guide how every parcel moves through our network — from when it enters the system to when it's delivered at the doorstep. Our data infrastructure is the backbone that powers everything from real-time operational decisions to strategic business insights to networks of autonomous AI agents.

We're looking for a Senior Analytics Engineer to sit within our Last Mile squad; our biggest and most data rich squad. Your role will help contribute to the success of the key agentic workflows that power our Last Mile marketplace.

You'll be joining at an exciting moment as we grow rapidly and expand our network across the UK.

## Who Will Thrive in this role?

Design core data models - Partner with engineers and business leaders within the Last Mile squad to design and build foundational data models that power analytics, data science and AI workflows

Build the foundations for our key AI systems - Our Last Mile marketplace is becoming increasingly agentic, you will be a key owner of the inputs that power these systems

Drive data quality - Implement testing, monitoring, and alerting to ensure our data is accurate, timely, and trustworthy

Collaborate - Work with team members across the business to understand their data needs and deliver solutions

Shape our practices — Help define and evolve our analytics engineering processes and standards as we scale

## What We're Looking For

5+ years of experience as an analytics engineer, data engineer, data analyst or similar role

Extensive experience with SQL, particularly with BigQuery and PostgreSQL

Demonstrated experience with dbt, with a track record of building and maintaining efficient, scalable, and reliable DAGs

Python proficiency for data manipulation, scripting, and automation

Experience with data visualisation tools such as Tableau, Looker, or similar

Experience in effective communication, particularly in gathering requirements and guiding non-technical stakeholders to the right data solutions

Familiarity with using AI tools to accelerate your workflow

Nice to Have:

Experience with orchestration tools like Dagster, Airflow, or similar

Experience building real-time or near-real-time data pipelines

Familiarity with machine learning workflows and feature engineering

Experience embedded in teams building customer facing products

Previous experience of learning and modelling an unfamiliar operational domain

A desire to see the real world impact of what you work on!
