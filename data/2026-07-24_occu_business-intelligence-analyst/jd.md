Business Intelligence Analyst

Occu
Location: Dublin, County Dublin, Ireland
Seniority Level: Mid-Senior
Employment Type: Full-time

The role focuses on developing Power BI dashboards and reporting across multiple business areas. The analyst will design, build and maintain Power BI dashboards across operations, leasing, asset and related functions.

Primary duties include:

Creating dashboards replacing legacy spreadsheets with single-source-of-truth equivalents

Building data models using Yardi and Power Query transformations

Developing DAX measures for metrics like void analysis, case ageing, yield and occupancy

Implementing data access controls and Row Level Security

Supporting stakeholder engagement and dashboard adoption

Essential Requirements

Hands-on expertise building Power BI dashboards end to end, from data model to publish

Advanced Power Query and DAX skills with complex transformations

Strong data modeling fundamentals including star schema, relationships and hierarchies

Working SQL knowledge

Experience implementing data access controls

Strong stakeholder communication abilities

Ability to work independently in fast-paced environments

Desirable Qualifications

Property/real estate or PRS sector experience

Familiarity with Yardi systems

Leasing and customer journey reporting background

GDPR-compliant reporting knowledge

Power BI certification
