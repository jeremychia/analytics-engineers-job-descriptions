Analytics Engineer

Celigo
Location: Hyderabad, Telangana, India
Employment Type: Full-time
Seniority Level: Mid-Senior level

Celigo seeks an Analytics Engineer to serve as the definitive owner of our business logic and the primary architect of our 'Gold' data layer. The role involves transforming complex datasets into production-ready tables while applying software engineering practices to data workflows.

Key Responsibilities

Develop and maintain scalable data models using dbt/Coalesce to transform raw data into clean, documented schemas

Collaborate with Data Engineering and Analytics teams on technical architecture

Implement automated testing, data documentation, and observability frameworks

Optimize SQL queries and warehouse configurations for performance and cost management

Maintain version control workflows, CI/CD pipelines, and coding standards

Support semantic layer and BI data catalogs for self-service exploration

Requirements

Education & Experience:
Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related quantitative field
3+ years in Analytics Engineering, Data Engineering, or technical Data Analyst roles
Expert-level SQL proficiency
Hands-on experience with dbt/Coalesce and cloud data warehouses (Snowflake or BigQuery)
Familiarity with Git, pull requests, and command-line interfaces

Desired Skills:
Python experience for data orchestration
Understanding of SaaS metrics (ARR, churn, financial reconciliation)
