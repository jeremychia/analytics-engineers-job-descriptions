## Data & Integration Engineer

Company: Marie-Stella-Maris
Location: Amsterdam, Tech department

### About the Company

Marie-Stella-Maris is a renowned lifestyle brand dedicated to making a positive impact on the world through premium home and body care products. Founded in 2011, the Dutch social enterprise has approximately 100 employees and focuses on providing clean drinking water and hygiene access globally.

### Position Overview

The role involves designing and maintaining data flows across retail, e-commerce, and back-office systems within the Business Technology team — characterized as business-focused rather than traditional IT support.

### Key Responsibilities

**Data Engineering & Pipelines:**
- Design and maintain pipelines between core systems (POS, e-commerce, ERP, OMS, CRM, BI)
- Ensure data quality, consistency, and availability
- Support and develop analytics data models
- Monitor and resolve data flow issues

**System Integrations:**
- Build integrations using APIs and Celigo middleware
- Develop data lake and BI environments (Fivetran, Databricks, dbt)
- Ensure robust, scalable integrations
- Support implementations and automation

**Collaboration & Delivery:**
- Work with Data Analysts, Product Owners, and operations teams
- Translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Support vendor partnerships and system rollouts

**Continuous Improvement:**
- Identify opportunities to simplify integration landscape
- Enhance performance and maintainability
- Establish standards and best practices

### Required Qualifications

- Data Engineering or Integration Specialist experience
- Hands-on API, webhook, and middleware integration experience (preferably Celigo)
- Data pipeline and analytics environment expertise (Fivetran, Databricks, dbt)
- Experience with SaaS platforms: Shopify, Salesforce, Power BI, PIM systems, POS solutions
- Multi-system and domain capability
- English fluency (Dutch preferred)
