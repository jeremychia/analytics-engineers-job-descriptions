Data Analytics Engineer - TIME dotCom Berhad

Position: Data Analytics Engineer
Company: TIME dotCom Berhad
Location: Shah Alam, Selangor, Malaysia
Seniority Level: Associate
Employment Type: Full-time

Job Overview
We are hiring a Data Analytics Engineer who bridges the gap between data engineering and data analysis. You'll own the transformation layer, ensuring data is clean, modeled, and ready for analysis using DBT and SQL.

Responsibilities
- Develop, optimize and maintain scalable data models using DBT.
- Write performant and reusable SQL queries for reporting and analytics.
- Translate complex business questions and operational requirements into meaningful, accessible and actionable data solutions.
- Build, enhance and maintain dashboards using a BI tool — preferably Looker.
- Work closely with stakeholders to gather requirements, understand business challenges and ensure data needs are met.
- Ensure the accuracy, consistency and reliability of data across datasets, reports and dashboards.

Requirements
- 5-7 years expert-level SQL skills and strong understanding of relational data modeling and data transformation best practices.
- Hands-on experience with DBT for building, maintaining and optimizing scalable transformation workflows and data models.
- Experience with at least one BI tool; Looker preferred.
- Strong communication skills, with the ability to listen, understand business problems deeply and avoid communication gaps between business and technical teams.
- Strong analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities and attention to detail, with a strong focus on data accuracy, validation and reliability.
- Experience/knowledge in Billing, Revenue Assurance & Fraud systems will be an added advantage.

Benefits
Comprehensive medical coverage, wellness support, learning opportunities, and professional development rewards are offered.
