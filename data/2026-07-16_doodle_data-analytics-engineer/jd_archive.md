- Collaborating with Product, Engineering, Marketing, Finance, and Leadership teams

- Advanced SQL skills for large analytical datasets

- Strong Python programming capabilities
- Knowledge of dimensional data modeling and analytics engineering
- Quality-focused mindset with testing and CI/CD experience
- Excellent communication skills across technical and non-technical stakeholders
