## About Acronis
Acronis is a global leader in cyber protection, delivering AI-powered protection for productive MSPs in a single, natively integrated platform that unifies operations management, cybersecurity, and data protection.

## Role summary
We are seeking an experienced BI leader to drive the end-to-end delivery of business intelligence solutions, establishing trusted KPIs and developing scalable semantic models that empower self-service reporting and analytics through Power BI. The role is responsible for ensuring data quality, operational excellence, and platform reliability through robust validation processes, monitoring, incident management, and well-defined operational procedures. In addition, the successful candidate will play a key role in modernizing the BI landscape through the adoption of Microsoft Fabric. As a people leader, this individual will manage and develop a small BI team, fostering a culture of collaboration, continuous improvement, and high performance while balancing delivery speed with long-term scalability and reliability.

## What you'll do
- Own end-to-end BI delivery (requirements, modeling, reporting) for prioritized business outcomes
- Define and maintain KPI/metric definitions, lineage, and reporting standards so stakeholders trust the numbers
- Build scalable semantic models and datasets, enabling self-service dashboards and ad-hoc analysis in Power BI
- Improve data quality and reliability through validation, monitoring, incident triage, and clear runbooks
- Lead and develop a team of 2 direct reports (prioritization, coaching, performance management, hiring/onboarding support)
- Drive modernization and Microsoft Fabric adoption, balancing delivery speed with reliability and operational excellence

## Who we're looking for
- 6+ years in BI/analytics engineering, with people-management experience
- Strong business acumen and stakeholder communication
- Strong SQL (T-SQL) and Microsoft BI stack: SQL Server, SSIS, SSAS (Tabular/Multidimensional), SSRS/Power BI Report Server, Power BI
- Data warehouse/mart design, ETL/ELT, and semantic modeling; exposure to Microsoft Fabric is a plus
- Agile delivery mindset with strong planning/prioritization, analytical problem-solving, and attention to detail
- Strong communication and facilitation skills; English (Upper-Intermediate) or higher

Desired (nice to have):
- Hands-on Microsoft Fabric experience (Lakehouse/Warehouse, Data Factory, semantic models) and adoption governance
- AI-assisted automation for BI/data work (testing, documentation, or pipeline scripting)
- Git + CI/CD for BI/data and scripting/automation (e.g., Azure DevOps, Jenkins, GitHub Actions, VS Code)

Please submit your resume and application in English.
