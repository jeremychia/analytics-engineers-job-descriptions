Role Overview

State Street Investment Management is a global leader in asset management, entrusted with more than $5.5 trillion in assets. For more than three decades, investors worldwide have benefited from our disciplined, precise investment process and powerful global platform offering access to every major asset class, capitalization range and style across the indexing and active spectrums. State Street IM is the investment management arm of State Street Corporation, one of the world's leading providers of financial services to institutions. 

Markets Intelligence & Strategic Insights (MISI) is the business unit within the Business Office at State Street Investment Management that supports the organization in reporting & analytics, data infrastructure/quality, sales enablement as well as Strategy and Planning. The vision of the MISI team is to be an indispensable information resource to help achieve desired goals and objectives. Our mission is to use information to provide a reliable foundation for decision-making, strategy development, opportunity realization and targeted business improvements.

The Analytics Engineer, Officer will serve as a hands-on technical contributor within the Markets Intelligence and Strategic Insights team. This individual will support the development, enhancement and maintenance of Tableau dashboards, analytical reporting solutions and underlying data infrastructure that enable informed decision-making across the business. The role will contribute to ETL pipeline development, data integration, data quality activities and the delivery of scalable analytical products. The successful candidate will work closely with colleagues across Strategy, Capital Markets, Technology and related business functions to translate business requirements into reliable data and reporting solutions.

Key Responsibilities

Contribute to the development and maintenance of analytical products across the full analytics lifecycle, including data preparation, data modelling, dashboard development, reporting and stakeholder delivery
Support the design, implementation and ongoing maintenance of ETL pipelines as we build out a single source of truth, helping to centralize, integrate and validate datasets within a scalable analytical infrastructure
Partner with business and sales teams to understand analytical requirements and develop reporting solutions that support commercial priorities, strategic planning and management decision-making
Develop, enhance and maintain Tableau dashboards that present business trends, performance drivers and key insights in a clear, accurate and user-friendly manner that enables decisioning
Perform data quality checks, validate transformation logic and support improvements to the analytical layer, escalating issues as appropriate and collaborating with upstream teams to support timely resolution
Document data definitions, assumptions, workflow logic and reporting methodologies to support consistency, transparency and repeatability across analytical outputs
Qualifications

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Engineering or a related field; equivalent practical experience will also be considered
2 to 5 years of relevant experience in analytics, business intelligence, analytics engineering, data engineering or a related discipline
Demonstrated experience contributing to analytical products, including data modelling, data transformation, dashboard development and stakeholder-facing reporting
Practical experience building, supporting or maintaining ETL pipelines and working with structured datasets in an enterprise environment
Strong SQL skills and working knowledge of Python; familiarity with Spark, Databricks or similar technologies is beneficial
Experience developing Tableau dashboards or comparable business intelligence reporting solutions
Strong analytical, problem-solving and communication skills, with the ability to work effectively with both technical and business stakeholders
Strong attention to detail, organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple priorities in a dynamic business environment
