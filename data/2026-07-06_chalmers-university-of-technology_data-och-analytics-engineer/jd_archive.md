# Data och Analytics Engineer - Chalmers University of Technology

Position: Data och Analytics Engineer
Location: Gothenburg, Västra Götaland County, Sweden
Employment Type: Full-time, permanent position
Seniority Level: Mid-Senior level
Application Deadline: August 31, 2026

Job Description
Chalmers is building "a new, modern platform for data and analytics" to support research, education, and administration. The role combines technical development with business understanding across data platforms, modeling, reporting, and visualization.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain reports, dashboards, and visualizations in Power BI
- Work with DAX, Power Query, report design, and semantic models
- Build shared structures for analysis and reporting focused on quality and auditability
- Collaborate with business stakeholders on requirements, definitions, testing, and validation
- Document datamodels, metrics, definitions, and report logic
- Work with AI-assisted development approaches using AI-agents

Required Qualifications:
- Minimum 5 years in BI, data analysis, reporting, or analytics engineering
- Deep practical experience with Power BI including development and datamodeling
- Understanding of semantic models, data modeling, and data quality
- Strong SQL knowledge
- Experience with modern data platforms (datalakes, lakehouses, ETL/ELT)
- Experience working directly with business on requirements, analysis, and validation
- Academic background in data, IT, systems science, statistics, or economics
- Swedish language certification C1 or equivalent

Preferred Qualifications:
- Databricks, Microsoft Fabric, or Snowflake experience
- Azure and cloud-based solutions
- Python for data work
- Power BI Service, deployment pipelines, workspace structures
- DevOps/GitHub, version control, CI/CD
- Infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Bicep)
- AI-agent tools
- Higher education or public sector experience

Workplace Benefits:
- Remote work flexibility
- Up to 7 weeks annual vacation
- Collective agreement with parental pay, sick pay supplements, pension, flexible hours
- Wellness stipend and 1 hour weekly wellness time

Application Process:
Submit CV and 1-2 page personal letter as PDFs. Optional: certificates or transcripts. Background checks may be conducted. Interviews ongoing during application period; position may fill before deadline.
