## Company Overview

Blue Orange Digital is a boutique data & AI consultancy that delivers enterprise-grade results. The firm specializes in designing and building modern data platforms, analytics, and ML/AI solutions for mid-market and enterprise clients in Private Equity, Financial Services, Healthcare, and Retail sectors. The organization uses technologies including Databricks, Snowflake, and dbt.

## Position Overview

This is a 26-week enterprise data warehouse implementation role in the commodities trading sector. The candidate will join from Week 6 through Week 22, focusing on the analytics and visualization layer of the project.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and construct Microsoft Fabric Semantic Models and Power BI datasets
- Define DAX measures, hierarchies, and relationships
- Develop production dashboards for Trader P&L, Global Position, VaR, and Financial Consolidation
- Engage directly with trading, risk, and finance stakeholders
- Optimize performance for complex financial datasets
- Establish naming conventions and semantic governance standards
- Collaborate with Data Engineers on data product readiness
- Support analytics enablement and UAT activities

## Requirements

- 7+ years in analytics engineering, BI development, or data analytics
- Deep expertise with Power BI (semantic models, DAX, report design, workspace management, RLS)
- Microsoft Fabric Power BI integration experience or SSAS/AAS background
- Strong analytical SQL capabilities
- Demonstrated stakeholder engagement experience in finance/trading environments
- Bachelor's degree in quantitative or technical field (or equivalent)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Commodity trading, financial risk, or P&L reporting experience
- Microsoft Power BI certifications (PL-300 or PL-400)
- DAX optimization expertise
- Fabric Lakehouse/Warehouse exposure

## Additional Information

Background checks may be required. Blue Orange Digital is an equal-opportunity employer. All application materials must be submitted in English.
