## Job Description Overview

This role focuses on creating trusted, analytics-ready data products that drive business decisions across Orange's consumer unit. The Analytics Engineer will design key metrics, build semantic models, and develop reliable datasets supporting self-serve analytics and AI capabilities.

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Design and maintain business-focused data products and KPIs
- Collaborate with Data Engineering on industrialization efforts
- Own KPI definitions and ensure data quality compliance
- Develop reusable data assets for self-serve analytics
- Prepare datasets for AI-driven analytics
- Partner with business stakeholders on analytics challenges

**Key Requirements:**
- Several years in analytics engineering, BI development, or data product design
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in engineering, data, statistics, or related field
- Strong SQL, data modeling, and cloud platform expertise (BigQuery preferred)
- Experience with semantic modeling and BI ecosystems
- Knowledge of data governance tools like DataGalaxy
- Proficiency with transformation frameworks (dbt)
- Understanding of generative AI and LLM-based querying
- "Fluent in English (knowledge of French is also desirable)"
- Telecom or commercial analytics background valued
