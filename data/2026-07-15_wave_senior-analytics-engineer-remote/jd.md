Senior Analytics Engineer at Wave Mobile Money Inc.

Location: Remote (must be based in talent hub countries: UK, Spain, USA east coast, Canada east coast, Kenya, Ghana, or African operating markets: Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso)

Salary: $123,500 - $176,400 USD annually (adjusted for local currency), plus equity package

Role Description

Wave seeks a senior analytics engineer to enhance data accessibility and reliability. The position focuses on optimizing the data estate for cost and performance efficiency while building robust analytical infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

- Dimensional design: Building facts, dimensions, and analytics tables while collaborating with domain stakeholders
- Snowflake management: Optimizing the data estate for cost and performance efficiency
- Developer tooling: Creating dbt macros and CI/CD checks to streamline analytics workflows
- ETL pipeline ownership: Managing pipeline health with on-call responsibilities

Core Requirements

- 5+ years as analytics or data engineer
- 3+ years with Snowflake, including performance optimization
- Dimensional modeling experience (minimum two marts built)
- Strong SQL, Python, and dbt proficiency
- Orchestration platform familiarity (Dagster/Airflow)

Benefits Highlights

- Subsidized health insurance
- 6 months of fully paid parental leave
- Flexible vacation (30-40 days typical)
- $10,000 annual charitable donation matching
