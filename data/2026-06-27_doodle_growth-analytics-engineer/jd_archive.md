# Growth Analytics Engineer - Doodle

**Company:** Doodle
**Location:** Berlin, London (Hybrid)
**Department:** Engineering

---

Doodle seeks a Growth Analytics Engineer for their Berlin/London offices (hybrid). The role focuses on building data foundations and enabling data-driven decisions across Growth, Product, Revenue, and Operations teams.

## Key Responsibilities

**Growth Analytics:** Own analytics across Doodle's self serve Product Led Growth business and Enterprise Revenue. Develop insights into acquisition, activation, monetization, retention, and customer behavior patterns.

**Business Intelligence:** Define, build, and maintain the KPIs that drive business performance. Design scalable dashboards enabling faster organizational decision-making.

**Data Products:** Build reusable datasets and reporting models eliminating manual work while improving data accessibility and reliability.

**Business Insights:** Conduct deep analyses on cohorts, lifecycle behavior, churn drivers, and pricing opportunities, translating findings into actionable recommendations.

**Experimentation:** Support growth experiments through measurement and analysis while evaluating AI initiative effectiveness.

## Requirements

- 5+ years in Analytics Engineering, Growth Analytics, or related SaaS roles
- Advanced SQL proficiency
- Python or R experience
- Familiarity with dbt, Redshift, GA4, HubSpot, Looker, or Tableau
- Knowledge of activation, retention, subscription metrics, and funnel analysis
- Strong cross-functional communication abilities
- Understanding of experimentation and statistical methods

## Ideal Candidate Profile

Think beyond dashboards and reports. Combine technical excellence with strong commercial thinking. Experience with experimentation analysis and cross-functional collaboration is valued.

## Hiring Process

Application review with BRYQ assessment, hiring manager interview, technical case study, cross-functional interview, executive and HR interviews, offer and reference checks.

## Important Note

We can only consider applicants who have the right to work in Germany or the United Kingdom. We are unable to sponsor visas.

## Company Context

Doodle is a B2B SaaS scheduling platform with 100+ employees across multiple countries, valuing diverse perspectives and quick implementation of ideas without bureaucratic delays.
