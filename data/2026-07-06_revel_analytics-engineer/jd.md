About REVEL
REVEL is described as "una startup española en fase scale-up que está redefiniendo cómo tener coche" (a Spanish scale-up startup redefining how to have a car). The company offers 100% online car rental with transparent digital experience. Their technological ecosystem includes web, native apps, proprietary APIs, and an internal ERP managing operations, logistics, risk, and finances. The tech team exceeds 30 people and will continue growing in 2026.

Responsibilities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Sales, Fleet, Customer Experience) and BI team to understand data needs, align metrics, business logic, and ensure data governance
- Develop and maintain analytical models in dbt, managing table structure, data lineage, and transformation efficiency
- Contribute to evolution and improvement of corporate dashboards in Power BI, ensuring consistency and quality
- Guide and support leaders and stakeholders on leveraging available data for business needs and objectives
- Generate clarity and solve complex business problems using data-driven approaches
- Perform ad-hoc analysis, identify trends and opportunities, deliver findings with concrete recommendations
- Collaborate with data team, developers, and analysts to evolve infrastructure, processes, and technical solutions

Required Qualifications
- Solid experience as Data Analyst or similar data analysis role
- Advanced SQL and Python proficiency for data analysis (Pandas, NumPy or similar)
- Efficient data transformation and modeling capabilities in SQL
- Power BI experience: dashboard development, semantic modeling, and DAX
- Experience with diverse data sources and formats (CSV, JSON, Parquet)
- Knowledge of data governance, data quality, and control methodologies
- Applied machine learning knowledge: churn models, lead scoring, demand forecasting, clustering, time series
- Optimization techniques for business decisions: cost reduction, resource allocation, operational constraints
- Strong statistical foundation and A/B testing design and analysis experience
- Autonomy and proactivity in problem-solving
- Fluent English

Nice-to-Have Skills
- dbt, AWS, and Airflow experience
- Startup environment and agile methodology experience
- Generative AI tools experience in analytical contexts
